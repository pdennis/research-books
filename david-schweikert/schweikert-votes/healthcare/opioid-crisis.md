# Heroin And Opioid Crisis

### Opioid Alternatives

**2019: Schweikert Voted For An Amendment To The FY 2020 Minibus That Provided Funding For Programs To Study Opioid Alternatives.** In June 2019, Schweikert voted for an amendment to the FY 2020 minibus that would, according to Congressional Quarterly, "allocate \$10 million for programs to implement or study opioid alternatives in emergency departments, from funds authorized by the bill for the Health and Human Services Department." The vote was on adoption of the amendment. The House adopted the amendment by a vote of 382-32. \[House Vote 322, [6/13/19](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2019/roll322.xml); Congressional Quarterly, [6/13/19](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2019/H/322?2); Congressional Actions, [H.Amdt. 339](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/116th-congress/house-amendment/339/actions); Congressional Actions, [H.R. 2740](https://www.congress.gov/bill/116th-congress/house-bill/2740/all-actions)\]
