# Emergency Medicine

### Emergency Medical Services For Children

**2024: Schweikert Voted To Authorize The Emergency Medical Services For Children Grant Program Through FY 2029.** In May 2024, Schweikert voted for , according to Congressional Quarterly, "the bill that would authorize the Emergency Medical Services for Children program through fiscal 2029 and increase the annual authorization for the program from \$22 million to \$24 million." The House passed the bill by a vote of 399 to 13. \[House Vote 208, [5/15/24](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2024/roll208.xml); Congressional Quarterly, [5/15/24](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2024/H/208?11); Congressional Actions, [H.R. 6960](http://www.congress.gov/bill/118th-congress/house-bill/6960/all-actions)\]
