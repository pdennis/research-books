# Ethics Reports

### Matt Gaetz

**2024: Schweikert Effectively Voted Against Releasing The Ethics Report On Matt Gaetz.** In December 2024, Schweikert voted for , according to Congressional Quarterly, the "motion to refer to the House Ethics Committee the resolution directing the Committee on Ethics of the House of Representatives to release to the public the committee's report on its investigation into allegations against former Representative Matt Gaetz." The House agreed to the motion by a vote of 206 to 198. \[House Vote 490, [12/5/24](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2024/roll490.xml); Congressional Quarterly, [12/5/24](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2024/H/490?4); Congressional Actions, [H.Res. 1608](https://www.congress.gov/bill/118th-congress/house-resolution/1608/all-actions)\]
