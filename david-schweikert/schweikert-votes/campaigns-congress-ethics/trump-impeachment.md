# Trump Impeachment

### December, 2017

**2017: Schweikert Effectively Voted Against Impeaching President Trump.** In December 2017, Schweikert voted to table a resolution that would have impeached President Trump. The vote was on a motion to table the resolution. The House tabled the resolution, effectively killing it, by a vote of 364 to 58. \[House Vote 658, [12/6/17](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2017/roll658.xml); Congressional Quarterly, [12/6/17](http://cq.com/vote/2017/H/658?0); Congressional Actions, [H. Res. 646](https://www.congress.gov/bill/115th-congress/house-resolution/646/all-actions)\]

### January 2018

**2018: Schweikert Effectively Voted Against Impeaching President Donald Trump.** In January 2018, Schweikert voted to table a resolution impeaching President Trump. The vote was on tabling the resolution, effectively killing it, by a vote of 355 to 66. \[House Vote 35, [1/19/18](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2018/roll035.xml); Congressional Quarterly, [1/19/18](http://www.cq.com/vote/2018/H/35?4); Congressional Actions, [H.Res. 705](https://www.congress.gov/bill/115th-congress/house-resolution/705/all-actions)\]
