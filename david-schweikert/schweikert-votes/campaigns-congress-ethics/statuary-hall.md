# Statuary Hall

### Honoring U.S. Territory Citizens

**2021: Schweikert Voted Against An Amendment That Would Allow Statues Honoring Citizens Of U.S. Territories In Statuary Hall.** In March 2021, Schweikert voted against en bloc amendments no.3 to the For The People Act which would, according to Congressional Quarterly, "permit the placement of statues honoring citizens of each U.S. territory in Statuary Hall." The vote was on adoption of amendments. The House adopted the amendments by a vote of 221-207. \[House Vote 55, [3/2/21](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2021/roll055.xml); Congressional Quarterly, [3/2/21](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2021/H/55?42); Congressional Actions, [H.Amdt. 20](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/117th-congress/house-amendment/20/actions?r=4&s=a); Congressional Actions, [H.R. 1](https://www.congress.gov/bill/117th-congress/house-bill/1/actions?q=%7b%22search%22%3A%5B%22hr1%22%5D%7d&r=1&s=2)\]
