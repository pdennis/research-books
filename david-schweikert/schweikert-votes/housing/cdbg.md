# Community Development Block Grant

### Funding

**2022: Schweikert Voted For An Amendment That Would Have Decreased Funding For The Community Development Block Grant By \$300 Million.** In July 2022, according to Congressional Quarterly, Schweikert voted for en bloc amendments no. 5 to the Transportation, Housing and Urban Development, and Related Agencies Appropriations Act, 2023, which would, in part, "decrease funding for the Community Development Block Grant by \$300 million." The vote was on the adoption of amendments. The House rejected the amendments by a vote 197-230. \[House Vote 371, [7/19/22](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2022/roll371.xml); Congressional Quarterly, [7/19/22](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2022/H/371?4); Congressional Actions, [H.Amdt. 297](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/117th-congress/house-amendment/297/actions?s=a&r=5); Congressional Actions, [H.R. 8294](https://www.congress.gov/bill/117th-congress/house-bill/8294/actions)\]
