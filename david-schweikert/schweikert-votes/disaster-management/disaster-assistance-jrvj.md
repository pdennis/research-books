# International Disaster Assistance

### Funding

**2024: Schweikert Voted To Reduce Funding For International Disaster Assistance By \$3.5 Billion.** In June 2024, Schweikert voted for , according to Congressional Quarterly, "amendment no. 54 that would reduce by \$3.5 billion funding for international disaster assistance and transfer the savings to the Spending Reduction Account." The vote was on the amendment. The underlying legislation was the FY 2025 State-Foreign Operations Appropriations. The House rejected the amendment by a vote of 101 to 312. \[House Vote 315, [6/27/24](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2024/roll315.xml); Congressional Quarterly, [6/27/24](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2024/H/315?11); Congressional Actions, [H.Amdt.1056](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/118th-congress/house-amendment/1056/actions); Congressional Actions, [H.R. 8771](http://www.congress.gov/bill/118th-congress/house-bill/8771/all-actions)\]
