# American Made Products

### Requiring That, When Practical, All Hydropower Generation Equipment Be Made In The U.S.

**2013: Schweikert Effectively Voted Against Requiring That, When Practical, All Materials Used For Conduit Hydropower Be Made In The United States.** In April 2013, Schweikert voted against a motion to recommit that would have, according to Congressional Quarterly, "require\[d\] that, when practicable, all materials used for conduit hydropower generation be manufactured in the United States." The underlying legislation was the Bureau of Reclamation Small Conduit Hydropower Development and Rural Jobs Act. The vote was on a motion to recommit. The House rejected the motion by a vote of 194 to 226. \[House Vote 95, [4/10/13](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2013/roll095.xml); Congressional Quarterly, [4/10/13](http://cq.com/vote/2013/H/95?51); Congressional Actions, [H.R. 678](https://www.congress.gov/bill/113th-congress/house-bill/678/all-actions)\]
