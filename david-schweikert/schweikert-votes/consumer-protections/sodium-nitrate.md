# Sodium Nitrate

### Ban

**2024: Schweikert Voted Against Banning Consumer Products With 10 Percent Or More By Weight Of Sodium Nitrate.** In May 2024, Schweikert voted against , according to Congressional Quarterly, "the bill that would ban, beginning 90 days after the bill's enactment, consumer products containing 10 percent or more by weight of sodium nitrate." The House passed the bill by a vote of 376 to 33. \[House Vote 206, [5/15/24](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2024/roll206.xml); Congressional Quarterly, [5/15/24](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2024/H/206?11); Congressional Actions, [H.R. 4310](http://www.congress.gov/bill/118th-congress/house-bill/4310/all-actions)\]
