# Denali Commission

### Funding

**2024: Schweikert Voted To Reduce Funding For The Denali Commission.** In July 2024, Schweikert voted for , according to Congressional Quarterly, "amendment no. 47 that would reduce funding for the Denali Commission by \$2 million and transfer the savings to the Spending Reduction Account." The vote was on the amendment. The underlying legislation was the FY 2025 Energy-Water appropriations. The House rejected the amendment by a vote of 134 to 283. \[House Vote 369, [7/23/24](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2024/roll369.xml); Congressional Quarterly, [7/23/24](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2024/H/369?3); Congressional Actions, [H.Amdt.1109](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/118th-congress/house-amendment/1109/actions); Congressional Actions, [H.R. 8997](https://www.congress.gov/bill/118th-congress/house-bill/8997/all-actions)\]
