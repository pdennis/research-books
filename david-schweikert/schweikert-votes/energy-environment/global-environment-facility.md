# Global Environment Facility

### Funding

**2024: Schweikert Voted To Reduce Funding For Educational And Cultural Exchange Programs By \$139.6 Million.** In June 2024, Schweikert voted for , according to Congressional Quarterly, "amendment no. 2 that would reduce by \$139.6 million funding for the Global Environment Facility." The vote was on the amendment. The underlying legislation was the FY 2025 State-Foreign Operations Appropriations. The House rejected the amendment by a vote of 180 to 227. \[House Vote 298, [6/27/24](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2024/roll298.xml); Congressional Quarterly, [6/27/24](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2024/H/298?11); Congressional Actions, [H.Amdt.1031](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/118th-congress/house-amendment/1031/actions); Congressional Actions, [H.R. 8771](http://www.congress.gov/bill/118th-congress/house-bill/8771/all-actions)\]
