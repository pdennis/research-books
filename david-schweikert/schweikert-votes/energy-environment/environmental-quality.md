# Council On Environmental Quality

### Chair Brenda Mallory

**2024: Schweikert Voted Against Reducing The Salary Of Brenda Mallory, Chair Of The Council On Environmental Quality, To \$1.** In July 2024, Schweikert voted against , according to Congressional Quarterly, "amendment no. 91 that would reduce to \$1 the salary of Council on Environmental Quality Chair Brenda Mallory." The vote was on the amendment. The underlying legislation was the FY 2025 Interior-Environment appropriations. The House rejected the amendment by a vote of 160 to 256. \[House Vote 398, [7/24/24](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2024/roll398.xml); Congressional Quarterly, [7/24/24](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2024/H/398?3); Congressional Actions, [H.Amdt.1174](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/118th-congress/house-amendment/1174/actions); Congressional Actions, [H.R. 8998](https://www.congress.gov/bill/118th-congress/house-bill/8998/all-actions)\]

### Funding

**2024: Schweikert Voted To Eliminate Funding For The Council On Environmental Quality.** In July 2024, Schweikert voted for , according to Congressional Quarterly, "amendment no. 83 that would eliminate the \$1 million provided for the Council on Environmental Quality and transfer the same amount to the spending reduction account." The vote was on the amendment. The underlying legislation was the FY 2025 Interior-Environment appropriations. The House rejected the amendment by a vote of 173 to 243. \[House Vote 396, [7/24/24](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2024/roll396.xml); Congressional Quarterly, [7/24/24](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2024/H/396?3); Congressional Actions, [H.Amdt.1168](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/118th-congress/house-amendment/1168/actions); Congressional Actions, [H.R. 8998](https://www.congress.gov/bill/118th-congress/house-bill/8998/all-actions)\]
