# Southwest Border Regional Commission

### Funding

**2024: Schweikert Voted To Reduce Funding For The Southwest Border Regional Commission.** In July 2024, Schweikert voted for , according to Congressional Quarterly, "amendment no. 51 that would reduce funding for the Southwest Border Regional Commission by \$2.5 million and transfer the savings to the Spending Reduction Account." The vote was on the amendment. The underlying legislation was the FY 2025 Energy-Water appropriations. The House rejected the amendment by a vote of 136 to 285. \[House Vote 373, [7/23/24](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2024/roll373.xml); Congressional Quarterly, [7/23/24](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2024/H/373?3); Congressional Actions, [H.Amdt.1113](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/118th-congress/house-amendment/1113/actions); Congressional Actions, [H.R. 8997](https://www.congress.gov/bill/118th-congress/house-bill/8997/all-actions)\]
