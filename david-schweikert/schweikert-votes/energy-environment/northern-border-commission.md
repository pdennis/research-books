# Northern Border Regional Commission

### Funding

**2024: Schweikert Voted To Reduce Funding For The Northern Border Regional Commission.** In July 2024, Schweikert voted for , according to Congressional Quarterly, "amendment no. 49 that would reduce funding for the Northern Border Regional Commission by \$21 million and transfer the savings to the Spending Reduction Account." The vote was on the amendment. The underlying legislation was the FY 2025 Energy-Water appropriations. The House rejected the amendment by a vote of 133 to 286. \[House Vote 371, [7/23/24](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2024/roll371.xml); Congressional Quarterly, [7/23/24](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2024/H/371?3); Congressional Actions, [H.Amdt.1111](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/118th-congress/house-amendment/1111/actions); Congressional Actions, [H.R. 8997](https://www.congress.gov/bill/118th-congress/house-bill/8997/all-actions)\]
