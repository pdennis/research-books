# Thin Blue Line Act

### Passage

**2017: Schweikert Voted To Force Juries And Courts To Consider It An Aggravating Factor That A Defendant Killed A Law Enforcement Officer When Determining If The Death Penalty Should Be Imposed.** In May 2017, Schweikert voted for the Thin Blue Line act. According to Congressional Quarterly, the legislation would have "require\[d\] courts and juries to consider if a defendant killed or attempted to kill a state law enforcement officer, local law enforcement officer or first responder as an aggravating factor when determining whether to impose the death sentence on a defendant." The House passed the bill by a vote of 271 to 143. The Senate took no substantive action on the legislation. \[House Vote 265, [5/18/17](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2017/roll265.xml); Congressional Quarterly, [5/18/17](http://cq.com/vote/2017/H/265?1); Congressional Actions, [H.R. 115](https://www.congress.gov/bill/115th-congress/house-bill/115/all-actions)\]
