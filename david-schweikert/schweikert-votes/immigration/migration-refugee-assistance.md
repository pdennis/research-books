# Migration And Refugee Assistance

### Funding

**2024: Schweikert Voted To Redirect \$500 Million In Funding For Migration And Refugee Assistance To International Narcotics Control And Law Enforcement.** In June 2024, Schweikert voted for , according to Congressional Quarterly, "amendment no. 36 that would reduce migration and refugee assistance funding by \$500 million and increase by the same amount funding for international narcotics control and law enforcement." The vote was on the amendment. The underlying legislation was the FY 2025 State-Foreign Operations Appropriations. The House adopted the amendment by a vote of 213 to 199. \[House Vote 312, [6/27/24](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2024/roll312.xml); Congressional Quarterly, [6/27/24](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2024/H/312?11); Congressional Actions, [H.Amdt.1051](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/118th-congress/house-amendment/1051/actions); Congressional Actions, [H.R. 8771](http://www.congress.gov/bill/118th-congress/house-bill/8771/all-actions)\]
