# Navy

### Littoral Combat Ship Retirement

**2022: Schweikert Voted Against An Amendment That Would Have Permitted The Navy To Retire 9 Littoral Combat Ships.** In July 2022, according to Congressional Quarterly, Schweikert voted against an amendment to the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2023, which would "allow the Navy to retire nine Littoral Combat Ships." The vote was on the adoption of an amendment. The House rejected the amendment by a vote 208-221. \[House Vote 319, [7/13/22](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2022/roll319.xml); Congressional Quarterly, [7/13/22](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2022/H/319?5); Congressional Actions, [H.Amdt. 256](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/117th-congress/house-amendment/256/actions?s=a&r=31); Congressional Actions, [H.R. 7900](https://www.congress.gov/bill/117th-congress/house-bill/7900/actions)\]
