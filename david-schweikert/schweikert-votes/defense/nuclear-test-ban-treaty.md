# Comprehensive Nuclear-Test-Ban Treaty Organization

### Funding

**2022: Schweikert Voted Against An Amendment That Removed The Limit On Funding For The Preparatory Commission For The Comprehensive Nuclear-Test-Ban Treaty Organization.** In July 2022, according to Congressional Quarterly, Schweikert voted against an amendment to the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2023, which would "repeal the restriction on funding for the Preparatory Commission for the Comprehensive Nuclear-Test-Ban Treaty Organization." The vote was on the adoption of an amendment. The House adopted the amendment by a vote 216-209. \[House Vote 320, [7/13/22](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2022/roll320.xml); Congressional Quarterly, [7/13/22](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2022/H/320?6); Congressional Actions, [H.Amdt. 257](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/117th-congress/house-amendment/257/actions?s=a&r=30); Congressional Actions, [H.R. 7900](https://www.congress.gov/bill/117th-congress/house-bill/7900/actions)\]
