# Armed Forces Members

### Military Service Standard Record

**2022: Schweikert Voted Against An Amendment That Established A Standard Record Of Military Service For All Armed Forces Members.** In July 2022, according to Congressional Quarterly, Schweikert voted against en bloc amendments no. 3 to the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2023, which would, in part, "establish a standard record of military service for all members of the armed forces." The vote was on the adoption of amendments. The House adopted the amendments by a vote 362-64. \[House Vote 333, [7/14/22](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2022/roll333.xml); Congressional Quarterly, [7/14/22](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2022/H/333?3); Congressional Actions, [H.Amdt. 270](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/117th-congress/house-amendment/270/actions?s=a&r=17); Congressional Actions, [H.R. 7900](https://www.congress.gov/bill/117th-congress/house-bill/7900/actions)\]
