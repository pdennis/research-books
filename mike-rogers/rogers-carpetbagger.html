[Lee County Property Tax Appraiser, 2023]

To Claim A Homestead Exemption In Lee County, Florida, The Property Must Be A “Permanent Residence.” According to the Lee County Property Appraiser, “Who is eligible to file for homestead exemption? Property owners must meet certain qualifications to be eligible for a homestead exemption: You must have legal title or a beneficial interest in real property as of January 1. The property must be your permanent residence as of January 1. You cannot claim a residency-based exemption in Florida, or in another state. If married, you may claim only one property as your permanent residence for the purpose of tax deduction. It is recommended that all owners of record, who have made the property their permanent residence, apply for the exemption.” [Lee County Property Tax Appraiser, 1/24/24]
