An estimated [_24,000 Michiganders_](https://www.jec.senate.gov/public/_cache/files/9cfdee90-dd29-41e8-97da-b5dcdc0317cf/updated-jec-fact-sheet-on-district-level-impacts-of-health-care-cuts.pdf) could lose their health insurance as a result of his vote.
-   Barrett‚Äôs vote also put hospitals [_in Michigan_](https://www.battlecreekenquirer.com/story/news/local/michigan/2025/07/09/cuts-to-medicaid-could-lead-to-4-michigan-hospitals-closing/84518782007/) at risk of closing.

¬†**Message:** Barrett voted to kick thousands of Michiganders off their health coverage all to give billionaires tax breaks.¬†

### **BARRETT SIDED WITH BIG PHARMA OVER MICHIGANDERS**

-   In Lansing, Barrett [_voted_](https://www.detroitnews.com/story/news/politics/michigan/2022/10/21/democrat-elissa-slotkin-republican-tom-barrett-michigan-7th-district-congress/69518573007/) to preserve Michigan‚Äôs unique law that [_shielded pharmaceutical companies_](https://www.detroitnews.com/story/news/local/michigan/2018/06/15/michigan-law-shields-opioid-lawsuits/649577002/) from being sued by Michigan families. ¬†**Message:** Barrett signed with big pharma over Michigan families who deserve cheaper prescription drugs and accountability.

- 	While running for Congress in 2024, Barrett [promised](https://www.freep.com/story/news/politics/elections/2024/09/09/barrett-hertel-us-house-race-michigan/75069362007/) he would not cut funding for Social Security or Medicare. **Message**: Barrett broke his promise and voted for massive cuts to health care services seniors rely on all to give tax breaks to billionaires.
