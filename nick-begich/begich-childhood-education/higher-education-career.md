# Higher Education and Early Career

### Summary

-   Nick Begich III earned a Bachelor of Business Administration from Baylor University and an MBA from Indiana University, later founding the technology development company FarShore Partners ([USA Today](https://www.usatoday.com/story/news/politics/2022/08/29/who-is-nick-begich-alaska-house-seat/10347524002/), [begich.house.gov](https://begich.house.gov/about), [Mat-Su Valley Frontiersman](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66FY-CB31-JBCN-44YT-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Early in his career, Begich worked for Ford Motor Company as a global technology manager and product market programmer ([Kodiak Daily Mirror](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6C5Y-7BH1-JBCN-3157-00000-00&context=1519360), [Alaska Dispatch News](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6643-YK31-JCBJ-Y471-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Nick Begich Sr., his grandfather, earned a BA from Saint Cloud State University in 1952 and an MA from the University of Minnesota in 1954, later pursuing graduate coursework at other institutions ([Wikipedia](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nick_Begich), [Kids.kiddle.co](https://kids.kiddle.co/Nick_Begich)).

-   After completing his academic studies, Nick Begich Sr. worked as an educator and administrator in Anchorage, Alaska public schools, including roles as guidance counselor and Superintendent of Schools at Fort Richardson ([Wikipedia](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nick_Begich), [Kids.kiddle.co](https://kids.kiddle.co/Nick_Begich)).

-   Nick Begich III once held over \$100,000 in student loans, which he has since paid off---highlighting past financial vulnerability ([Alaska Dispatch News](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:65GC-35P1-DYTM-N00H-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   In 1994, Nick Begich (possibly Nick Begich Jr.) received an honorary Doctor of Medicine from the Open International University for Complementary Medicines in Sri Lanka ([Earthpulse Press](https://web.archive.org/web/20110518105035/http://www.earthpulse.com/src/category.asp?catid=13)).

### Colleges and Universities

#### Degrees Pursued

**Nick Begich Earned Business Degrees And Founded Tech Company** According to USA Today, "Nicholas Begich III, earned a bachelor of business degree from Baylor University and an MBA from Indiana University. He founded FarShore Partners, a technology development company, according to Ballotpedia." \[USA Today, [8/29/22](https://www.usatoday.com/story/news/politics/2022/08/29/who-is-nick-begich-alaska-house-seat/10347524002/)\]

**1952 & 1954: Nick Begich Earned Bachelor's And Master's Degrees** According to Wikipedia, "He earned a Bachelor of Arts from Saint Cloud State University in 1952 and a Master of Arts from the University of Minnesota in 1954. He took graduate courses at the University of Colorado Boulder and University of North Dakota." \[Wikipedia, [6/9/24](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nick_Begich)\]

**Nick Begich Earned Degrees From St. Cloud State University And University Of Minnesota** According to Kids.kiddle.co, "He earned a Bachelor of Arts from Saint Cloud State University in 1952 and a Master of Arts from the University of Minnesota 1954. He took graduate courses at the University of Colorado Boulder and University of North Dakota." \[Kids.kiddle.co, [10/18/24](https://kids.kiddle.co/Nick_Begich)\]

**1952--1954: Nick Begich Sr. Earned His BA And MA In Minnesota** According to Wikipedia, "He earned a Bachelor of Arts from Saint Cloud State University in 1952 and a Master of Arts from the University of Minnesota in 1954." \[Wikipedia, [1/19/25](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nick_Begich_Sr.)\]

**2022: Letter Writer Jim and Faye Palin Noted Nick Begich's Educational and Professional Background** According to a letter to the editor published in Mat-Su Valley Frontiersman, "Following high school, he earned a BBA at Baylor University then an MBA at Indiana University. \[...\] He worked for Ford Motor company for several years and then started his own business. He is the Founder and Executive Chairman of FarShore Partners, a full-service custom software and product development design agency which provides technical expertise locally and internationally." \[Letter to the Editor - Mat-Su Valley Frontiersman, [9/22/22](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66FY-CB31-JBCN-44YT-00000-00&context=1519360)\]

#### Institutions Attended

**Nick Begich Graduated From Baylor University And Indiana University** According to begich.house.gov, "Nick pursued higher education in the Lower 48, graduating from Baylor University with a Bachelor of Business Administration in Entrepreneurship, and later completing his Master of Business Administration at Indiana University." \[begich.house.gov, [1/2/25](https://begich.house.gov/about)\]

**Nick Begich III Earned Business Degrees From Baylor University And Indiana University** According to Alaska Dispatch News, "He ended up at Baylor University in Texas, graduating with a degree in business. He later got a master's degree in business administration from Indiana University and worked for Ford Motor Company." \[Alaska Dispatch News, [8/5/22](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6643-YK31-JCBJ-Y471-00000-00&context=1519360)\]

**Nick Begich III Detailed Personal and Educational Background in 2024** According to Kodiak Daily Mirror, "Nick III attended Baylor University and took a job in industrial relations. \[...\] That demand was impetus for him to earn an MBA at Indiana University. \[...\] Nick interned at Ford Motor Company in Detroit, eventually landing a job with Ford in nearby Dearborn. He worked as a global technology manager and product market programmer for Ford's minivan program." \[Kodiak Daily Mirror, [6/4/24](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6C5Y-7BH1-JBCN-3157-00000-00&context=1519360)\]

**1994: Nick Begich Received Honorary Doctorate From Open International University For Complementary Medicines In Sri Lanka** According to Chemtrail Crimes, "Begich received Doctor of Medicine (Medicina Alternitiva), honoris causa, for independent work in health and political science, from The Open International University for Complementary Medicines, Colombo, Sri Lanka, in November 1994." \[Chemtrail Crimes (YouTube), [4/13/23](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ku4RCg52ihk&feature=youtu.be)\]

#### Internships and Work Experience

**2022: Begich Stated He Had Over \$100,000 In Student Loans, Since Paid Off** According to Alaska Dispatch News, "He said his family 'struggled' when he was growing up, and at one point, Begich said, he had more than \$100,000 in student loans, since paid off." \[Alaska Dispatch News, [5/17/22](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:65GC-35P1-DYTM-N00H-00000-00&context=1519360)\]
