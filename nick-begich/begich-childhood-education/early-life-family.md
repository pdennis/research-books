# Early Life and Family Background

### Summary

-   Nick Begich III was born in Anchorage, Alaska, but was raised in Florida by his maternal grandparents after his parents divorced ([Anchorage Daily News](https://www.adn.com/politics/2023/07/13/republican-nick-begich-to-challenge-incumbent-democrat-mary-peltola-in-alaskas-us-house-race/), [Mat-Su Valley Frontiersman](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66FY-CB31-JBCN-44YT-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   His maternal grandparents, described as Bible Belt Republicans from Missouri, played a significant role in instilling conservative Christian values during his upbringing ([Peninsula Clarion](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:64HW-GPH1-JD2C-J4F2-00000-00&context=1519360), [Mat-Su Valley Frontiersman](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66FY-CB31-JBCN-44YT-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Begich has emphasized his return to Alaska as an adult, highlighting his commitment to the state and noting he and his family have lived there for over 20 years ([Kodiak Daily Mirror](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6C5Y-7BH1-JBCN-3157-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Despite being part of the prominent Begich political family---mostly Democrats---Nick Begich III identifies as a conservative Republican, setting himself apart from his relatives' political affiliations ([Anchorage Daily News](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DT9-PNP3-RTC3-S0HR-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Political opponents and media have occasionally questioned Begich's Alaska ties, arguing that being raised outside of Alaska challenges his authenticity as an Alaskan candidate ([Juneau Empire](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D9H-H231-JD2C-J1FS-00000-00&context=1519360), [The Daily Sitka Sentinel](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DB3-H3N1-JBCN-32G9-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Begich has spoken about his challenging childhood in Florida, describing financial instability and a motivation to provide more stability for his own family ([Alaska Dispatch News](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6643-YK31-JCBJ-Y471-00000-00&context=1519360)).
