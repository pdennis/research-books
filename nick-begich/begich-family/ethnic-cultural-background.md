# Ethnic and Cultural Background

### Summary

-   Nick Begich Sr. was born in 1932 in Eveleth, Minnesota ([Wikipedia](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nick_Begich)).

-   Both of his parents were of Croatian descent, with his father, John Begich (né Begić), originally from Podlapača, Udbina, Croatia, and his mother, Anna (née Martinić), also of Croatian heritage ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nick_Begich_Sr.), [Kids.kiddle.co](https://kids.kiddle.co/Nick_Begich)).

-   The Begich family's Croatian roots shaped their cultural background and likely influenced Nick Begich Sr.'s upbringing.

-   There are no indications of additional ethnic groups beyond Croatian heritage in the available sources.
