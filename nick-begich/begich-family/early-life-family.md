# Early Life and Family Background

### Summary

-   Nick Begich III's family has experienced tragedy related to air travel, a loss shared by many Alaskans ([Anchorage Daily News](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6F42-H9F3-RRJ4-S4YM-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   His grandfather, U.S. Rep. Nick Begich, disappeared on a campaign flight in 1972, an event that deeply impacted the family ([Anchorage Daily News](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6F43-H1D3-RXTD-V2DP-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Nick Begich III has publicly referenced his family's aviation tragedy, using it as context for his commitment to improving air travel safety.

-   The family's high-profile history and past tragedy could present a vulnerability, potentially attracting public and media scrutiny during campaigns.
