-   Rep. Nick Begich used and defended strong and sometimes obscene language, including calling President Biden a "son of a bitch," citing the need to "shout to be heard" in Congress ([Anchorage Daily News](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DXS-7BB3-RSRY-B4WW-00000-00&context=1519360)).
