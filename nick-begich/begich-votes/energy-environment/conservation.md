# Conservation

### Nutria Eradication

**2025: Begich Voted To Reauthorize A Nutria Eradication Program Aimed At Conserving Marshland.** In February 2024, Begich voted for , according to Congressional Quarterly, "the bill that would reauthorize, through fiscal 2030, the 2003 law that established a program to eradicate or control nutria and restore marshland damaged by the semiaquatic rodent." The House passed the bill by a vote of 361 to 56. \[House Vote 29, [[2/4/25]{.underline}](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2025/roll029.xml); Congressional Quarterly, [[2/4/25]{.underline}](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2025/H/29?4); Congressional Actions, [[H.R. 776]{.underline}](https://www.congress.gov/bill/119th-congress/house-bill/776/all-actions)\]
