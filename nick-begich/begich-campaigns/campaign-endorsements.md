# Campaign Management and Endorsements

### Summary

-   In 2021, Nick Begich III hired Truman Reed, who previously managed Don Young's 2020 campaign, as his own campaign manager ([Alaska Dispatch News](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63X6-F5R1-DYTM-N31V-00000-00&context=1519360), 10/22/21).

-   Truman Reed continued to serve as Begich's campaign manager through 2022, maintaining strong campaign ties to Don Young ([Alaska Dispatch News](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66R5-M5K1-DYTM-N14F-00000-00&context=1519360), 10/27/22).

-   Nick Begich III reportedly acted as campaign chairman for Don Young's 2020 reelection effort, showcasing a close political relationship ([Peninsula Clarion](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63XY-K4D1-F0HF-82NS-00000-00&context=1519360), 10/25/21).

-   The shared campaign staff and leadership highlight continuing alliances between Begich and Young, potentially appealing to Young's supporters.

-   These close connections could be viewed as a vulnerability by voters seeking greater independence or change from established political figures.

### Campaign Team and Strategy

**2021: Nicholas Begich III Reported Serving As Don Young's Campaign Chairman In 2020** According to Peninsula Clarion, "Begich also has close ties to Young, serving as a chairman of the congressman's campaign in 2020, when Young defeated independent Alyse Galvin." \[Peninsula Clarion, [10/25/21](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63XY-K4D1-F0HF-82NS-00000-00&context=1519360)\]
