# Environmental Protection and Climate Policy

### Summary

-   Frequently criticizes efforts to address climate change, labeling social cost of carbon models and climate policy as "weaponized" by political opponents, which could alienate voters concerned about environmental protection ([Daily Sitka Sentinel](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CW3-8TR1-DXVP-T4S5-00000-00&context=1519360), [Juneau Empire](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CV9-B7N1-F0HF-81C1-00000-00&context=1519360))

-   Openly questions the scientific and economic methodologies that link fossil fuel production to climate change impacts, which could be portrayed as climate change denial ([Mat-Su Valley Frontiersman](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CV8-PY41-DXVP-T11W-00000-00&context=1519360))

-   Has stated that Congress cannot meaningfully address climate change, emphasizing a lack of viable legislative solutions and downplaying the importance of national climate action ([Kodiak Daily Mirror](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66J8-YGG1-DXVP-V4S7-00000-00&context=1519360), [Fairbanks Daily News-Miner](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66JT-K6H1-DXVP-V0NM-00000-00&context=1519360))

-   Argues that climate-focused regulations harm the economy and working families, potentially making him appear more focused on industry than on long-term environmental or public health ([Anchorage Daily News](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DV6-KB93-S4RS-V14M-00000-00&context=1519360))

-   Opposed high-profile conservation actions by the Biden administration, such as protecting 28 million acres from development, which environmental groups or moderate voters could see as anti-conservation ([Juneau Empire](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CV9-B7N1-F0HF-81C1-00000-00&context=1519360))
