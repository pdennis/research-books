# Overview of Personal Litigation Involving Nick Begich

### Summary

-   In June 2022, Nick Begich's campaign became involved in legal action concerning a ballot dispute in the special election for Alaska's U.S. House seat ([Fairbanks Daily News-Miner](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:65S3-59X1-DXVP-V3F6-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Begich's campaign supported the Alaska Division of Elections' interpretation regarding ballot eligibility, which led to intervention in the case and effectively challenged Tara Sweeney's potential candidacy ([Peninsula Clarion](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:65SJ-H3D1-F0HF-8065-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Tara Sweeney and her representatives publicly criticized Begich for seeking legal measures that would block her from appearing on the ballot, arguing that it would limit voter choices ([Anchorage Press](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:65T5-17N1-DXVP-V1BH-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Public commentary, including a letter to the editor, accused Begich of using his resources to hire legal counsel specifically to prevent Sweeney from running in the election ([Alaska Dispatch News](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:65T9-5YF1-DYTM-N4B1-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   This litigation exposed Begich to criticism and the perception that his actions could be reducing electoral options for Alaskan voters, potentially creating a vulnerability in his political standing ([Anchorage Press](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:65TC-0FB1-JBCN-4104-00000-00&context=1519360)).

### Types of Legal Actions

#### Civil Lawsuits

**June 2022: A Spokeswoman For Tara Sweeney Claimed Nick Begich Had Involved Legal Counsel In Ballot Dispute** According to Fairbanks Daily News-Miner, "Saying that 'it looks like Nick Begich has already called in the lawyers,' a spokeswoman for Tara Sweeney said Wednesday the U.S. House candidate does not plan to challenge a decision by election officials not to place her name on the August ballot." \[Fairbanks Daily News-Miner, [6/22/22](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:65S3-59X1-DXVP-V3F6-00000-00&context=1519360)\]

**June 2022: Begich Campaign Supported Election Division's Interpretation On Ballot Replacement** According to Peninsula Clarion, "In a statement, she criticized Begich, whose campaign agreed with the division's interpretation and intervened in the case. She said Begich sought legal action 'to block the advancement of my candidacy to limit the choices for Alaskans.'" \[Peninsula Clarion, [6/24/22](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:65SJ-H3D1-F0HF-8065-00000-00&context=1519360)\]

**June 2022: Begich Campaign Sought Legal Action To Block Sweeney's Candidacy In Special Election** According to Anchorage Press, "In the run-up to the court's decision, Sweeney took special aim at the Begich campaign for taking legal action against her status on the ballot. 'It is concerning to me that Nick Begich sought immediate legal action to block the advancement of my candidacy to limit the choices for Alaskans,' she said in a prior statement." \[Anchorage Press, [6/27/22](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:65T5-17N1-DXVP-V1BH-00000-00&context=1519360)\]

**2022: Letter Writer Douglas Smith Claimed Nick Begich Attempted to Block Tara Sweeney from Running** According to a letter to the editor published in Alaska Dispatch News, "Nick Begich has used his money to hire an attorney in an attempt to block Tara Sweeney from running against him to replace Congressman Don Young." \[Letter to the Editor - Alaska Dispatch News, [6/28/22](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:65T9-5YF1-DYTM-N4B1-00000-00&context=1519360)\]

**2022: Republican Nick Begich Sought Legal Action To Block Tara Sweeney's Candidacy** According to The Anchorage Press, 'Nick Begich sought immediate legal action to block the advancement of my candidacy to limit the choices for Alaskans' that she's already been using but it could very well cost Begich the special election. \[Anchorage Press (Alaska), [6/29/22](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:65TC-0FB1-JBCN-4104-00000-00&context=1519360)\]
