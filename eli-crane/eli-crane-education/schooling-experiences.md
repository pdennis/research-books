# Schooling and Academic Experiences

### Summary

-   Eli Crane graduated from Cibola High School in 1998 ([Geneastar](https://en.geneastar.org/genealogy/craneelijah/eli-crane)).

-   He studied sociology at Arizona Western College and the University of Arizona ([Geneastar](https://en.geneastar.org/genealogy/craneelijah/eli-crane)).

-   Crane was in his senior year at the University of Arizona when the September 11 attacks occurred ([Maricopa Monitor](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:638J-HS91-DXVP-V3TY-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Motivated by 9/11, Crane left college before graduating to join the U.S. Navy ([Maricopa Monitor](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:638J-HS91-DXVP-V3TY-00000-00&context=1519360); [Geneastar](https://en.geneastar.org/genealogy/craneelijah/eli-crane)).

-   He did not complete a college degree due to his decision to enlist in the military ([Maricopa Monitor](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CBD-M191-JBCN-3479-00000-00&context=1519360)).

### Primary and Secondary Education

#### Schools Attended

**1998: Eli Crane Graduated From Cibola High School** According to Geneastar, "Crane graduated from Cibola High School in 1998 and studied sociology at Arizona Western College and the University of Arizona." \[Geneastar, [1/1/23](https://en.geneastar.org/genealogy/craneelijah/eli-crane)\]

### Education Challenges and Decisions

#### Transition to Military Service

**2001-2014: Eli Crane Served In The U.S. Navy, Including As A SEAL** According to Geneastar, "Crane stated that one week after the September 11 attacks, he dropped out of college and served in the United States Navy from 2001 to 2014. He was a member of the United States Navy SEALs and was deployed five times. Three of the five deployments were with the SEALS." \[Geneastar, [1/1/23](https://en.geneastar.org/genealogy/craneelijah/eli-crane)\]
