# Advocacy and Support for Veterans in Business

### Championing Veteran Entrepreneurship

#### Involvement with Veteran Entrepreneurs

**Eli Crane's Company Bottle Breacher Employed And Aided Veterans As Of 2021** According to Arizona Republic, "The company remains in business and places an emphasis on employing and aiding veterans." \[Arizona Republic (Phoenix), [7/22/21](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:636D-MH31-JC3H-03Y5-00000-00&context=1519360)\]

**2023: Eli Crane Claimed to Have Prioritized Veteran Employment in His Business After Military Service** According to an opinion piece by Eli Crane in Casa Grande Dispatch, "Following my service in the Navy, I harnessed my military background and life experience to start a small business with my wife Jen that employed and gave back to veterans across the country." \[Eli Crane - Casa Grande Dispatch, [10/6/23](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:69BH-SH51-JBCN-30DY-00000-00&context=1519360)\]
