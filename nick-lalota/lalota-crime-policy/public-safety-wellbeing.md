# Stance on Public Safety and Community Wellbeing

### Summary

> * Congressman Nick LaLota was briefed by the FBI about the investigation into the New Orleans attack, indicating active lawmaker involvement in public safety issues ([CBS - 3 WREG](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DTG-P0Y3-RSH5-54C2-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * LaLota publicly acknowledged the recency of the New Orleans incident, describing it as "very fresh," which demonstrates transparency and awareness of current security events ([CBS - 3 WREG](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DTG-P0Y3-RSH5-54C2-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * He cautioned that immediate answers should not be expected following major incidents like the Las Vegas explosion, emphasizing a commitment to thorough investigations and fact-finding ([CBS - 3 WREG](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DTG-P0Y3-RSH5-54C2-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * The statements point to a stance that prioritizes patient, evidence-based approaches to public safety concerns, with a focus on keeping the public informed as details become available.
> * A potential vulnerability is the admitted uncertainty and lack of immediate answers in the days following such incidents, which may affect public confidence or heighten anxieties during ongoing investigations.
