# Financial Transparency and Public Disclosures

### Summary

> * Nick LaLota faced scrutiny over his financial disclosures, with the FEC raising multiple questions about his filings and advocacy group End Citizens United alleging he failed to fully disclose consulting clients, assets, and certain spousal and retirement holdings ([Newsday, 4/23/23](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:682W-V611-DY2H-J2H5-00000-00&context=1519360); [Newsday, 6/29/23](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:68K5-D3T1-DY2H-J155-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * LaLota initially misfiled his 2022 disclosure under his first name, complicating public access, and did not respond to press questions about the error ([Newsday, 6/26/23](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:68JH-GH41-DY2H-J3TD-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Following complaints, LaLota submitted a revised, more detailed disclosure in August 2023, correcting errors and adding previously omitted information, including a significant home mortgage and detailed retirement account information ([Newsday, 8/15/23](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:68Y6-1N31-DY2H-J4YF-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * LaLota has twice filed for extensions to submit his annual financial disclosures, both for 2023 and 2024, which could raise questions about timeliness and compliance with disclosure requirements ([Newsday, 6/26/23](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:68JH-GH41-DY2H-J3TD-00000-00&context=1519360); [Newsday, 5/30/24](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6C4T-KTR1-DY2H-J14D-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * His campaign fundraising has been strong, reporting $1.6 million raised since early 2023, including large transfers from prominent GOP fundraising groups ([City & State, 2/5/24](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6B8G-35X1-DXVP-T1RV-00000-00&context=1519360); [Newsday, 10/17/24](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D6N-HBT1-DY2H-J368-00000-00&context=1519360)).
