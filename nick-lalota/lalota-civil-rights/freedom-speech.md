### Position on Political Discourse

**2017: Nick LaLota Ran For Amityville Mayor Against Dennis Siry** According to Amityville Record (New York), "the suspension was actually a retaliatory measure for him exercising his First Amendment rights of freedom of speech and freedom of political expression 'for supporting a political candidate who opposed their (the department's) preferred candidate for mayor, Dennis Siry,' who eventually won the election."
