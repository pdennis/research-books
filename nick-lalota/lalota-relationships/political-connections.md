# Connections with Political Parties and Groups

### Summary

> * LaLota has consistently aligned himself with the Republican and Conservative parties, receiving endorsements and official backing over numerous election cycles, which could be a liability with swing or independent voters ([Suffolk Times](https://suffolktimes.timesreview.com/2022/02/former-suffolk-boe-commissioner-nick-lalota-announces-campaign-for-congress-zeldin-picks-lieutenant-governor/), [Massapequa Post](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5YDC-TKW1-DXVP-V2YR-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * His public endorsement of Donald Trump in January 2024 and close association with the party’s right wing have drawn criticism, especially from opponents highlighting his alignment with Trump over bipartisan interests ([East Hampton Star](https://www.easthamptonstar.com/government/2024122/lalota-lines-behind-trump), [New York Times](https://www.nytimes.com/2024/08/19/nyregion/avlon-lalota-residency-congress.html)).
> * Despite touting a "common-sense conservative" and bipartisan image, LaLota votes with the Republican Party over 95% of the time, raising questions about the depth of his bipartisan credentials ([Newsday](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:68SP-MWB1-JBKF-V1X1-00000-00&context=1519360), [New York Times](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CRN-MNX1-DXY4-X0K7-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Participation in both bipartisan caucuses and strongly conservative groups (e.g., Republican Study Committee, Problem Solvers Caucus) may lead to perceptions of inconsistency or opportunism in appealing to multiple constituencies ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nick_LaLota), [City & State](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:68SN-MPT1-DXVP-T0X2-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Close involvement and leadership roles in local party organizations, such as the West End Republican Club and Babylon Republican Committee, could expose him to criticism if past local party maneuvers or controversies resurface ([Amityville Record](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:60KW-74N1-DXVP-V0PX-00000-00&context=1519360), [Babylon Beacon](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:634R-5CC1-JBCN-4188-00000-00&context=1519360)).
