Political Vulnerabilities

Tillis Had No Spine And Was A Flip-Flopper 

  • Tillis wrote a column in the Washington Post announcing his disapproval of trump’s emergency declaration only to vote in support of the declaration three weeks later.
  • Tillis claimed he wanted to protect healthcare coverage for people with pre-existing conditions, but supported legislation that would remove those protections.
  • Despite opposing a casino, the South Carolina-based Catawba Nation, wanted to build in North Carolina while he was a state legislator, Tillis sponsored legislation to authorize the casino as a member of the Senate.
  • Tillis voted for Hegseth’s confirmation despite concerns.
  • Tillis claimed that shutting down USAID was a mistake but later defended the move.
  • Tillis excused Trump’s executive overreach despite previously calling for Congress to claw their power back.

Tillis Was An Anti-Abortion Extremist 

  • Tillis claimed that he was “proudly pro-life.”
  • Tillis stated that one of his goals as speaker was to limit abortions in North Carolina.
  • Tillis praised the Dobbs decision.
  • Tillis voted to restrict abortion access and opposed bills that would’ve expanded abortion access.
  • Tillis-led House backed an anti-abortion bill that risked closure of the majority of North Carolina’s abortion clinics.
  • Tillis opposed federal funds going towards abortion healthcare.
  • Tillis supported a personhood amendment

Tillis Was Bad For Affordable Healthcare 

  • Tillis opposed the Affordable Care Act and voted 20 times to repeal parts or the entirety of the ACA.
  • Tillis opposed the Inflation Reduction Act which would’ve lowered prescription drug costs.
  • Tillis watered down a bill that was meant to crack down on Patent “thicketing.”
  • Tillis voted for budgets that would’ve cut Medicare and supported cutting Medicaid.

 

Tillis Supported Tax Cuts For The Rich 

  • Tillis supported Trump’s 2017 tax cuts for the rich.
  • Tillis supported making Trump’s 2017 tax cuts permanent.

 

Tillis Supported Trump’s Tariffs 

  • Tillis supported Trump’s tariffs.
  • Tillis opposed legislation that would’ve blocked Trump’s tariffs.

 

Tillis Was Bad For Workers

  • Tillis opposed Unions and pledged that North Carolina Would Be “The Least Unionized State In The United States.”
  • Tillis co-sponsored national right to work legislation.
  • Tillis opposed raising the minimum wage.

 

Tillis Was Weak On Defending Social Security 

  • Tillis suggested cutting entitlement programs to pay for tax reform.
  • Tillis voted against a bill that restored Social Security benefits for public sector employees.
  • Tillis supported raising the retirement age.
  • Tillis told Bisignano not to “pull any punches” on decisions about closing Social Security offices.
