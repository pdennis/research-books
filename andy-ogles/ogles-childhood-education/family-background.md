# Family Background and Early Life

### Summary

> * Andy Ogles was born and raised in Williamson County, Tennessee, with family roots said to date back to the early settlers of East Tennessee ([Columbia Daily Herald](https://www.columbiadailyherald.com/story/news/2022/02/17/no-newcomers-maury-mayor-andy-ogles-argues-local-roots-should-stay-5th-district-after-ortagus-bid/6789178001/)).
> * He grew up in Franklin and later moved with his family to Maury County, where he highlighted the area's "small town charm" and traditional values ([Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63B2-6YW1-DXVP-V42S-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Ogles has frequently emphasized his local roots as central to his political identity, notably stating local ties are important for representing Tennessee's 5th District ([Columbia Daily Herald](https://www.columbiadailyherald.com/story/news/2022/02/17/no-newcomers-maury-mayor-andy-ogles-argues-local-roots-should-stay-5th-district-after-ortagus-bid/6789178001/), [Chattanooga Times Free Press](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:64WY-4TW1-JBP6-R26N-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * His historic ties are primarily to Williamson and Maury Counties, with limited connections to Nashville itself ([Tennessean](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66XG-JM71-DXGX-63J4-00000-00&context=1519360), [Columbia Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6706-B9D1-JB1V-G355-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Ogles has cited family traditions such as attending Mule Day and formative experiences with his grandparents and as an Eagle Scout as influential in his upbringing ([Tennessean](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:653H-C7D1-DYJJ-P101-00000-00&context=1519360), [Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5WW6-6R01-DXVP-V4R7-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * There are reported vulnerabilities regarding Ogles' personal narrative, with investigations suggesting parts of his life story may contain exaggerations ([NewsChannel 5](https://www.newschannel5.com/news/newschannel-5-investigates/businessman-economist-cop-international-sex-crimes-expert-the-stories-of-congressman-andy-ogles)).
