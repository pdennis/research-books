# Business and Nonprofit Roles

### Summary

> * Andy Ogles owned and operated restaurant and real estate businesses for over a decade early in his career, including Daylight Donuts in Franklin, TN ([Tennessean](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:497R-H670-01D1-84DK-00000-00&context=1519360); [Club for Growth Foundation](https://clubforgrowthfoundation.org/fellow/andy-ogles/)).
> * He held leadership roles in the nonprofit sector, notably as a part-time employee (not COO as sometimes claimed) of Abolition International, making $4,000 while assisting anti-trafficking efforts ([Tennessee Tribune](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:67ND-RNB1-JBHT-D1XK-00000-00&context=1519360); [Columbia Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CK0-JPT1-JB1V-G465-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Ogles served as executive director of the Laffer Center, a supply-side economic organization, and was also a fellow of the Club for Growth Foundation ([Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:62F3-CKN1-JBCN-41C0-00000-00&context=1519360); [Club for Growth Foundation](https://clubforgrowthfoundation.org/fellow/andy-ogles/)).
> * Investigations have challenged Ogles' claims regarding his roles and expertise, including assertions of being a nationally recognized tax and healthcare expert, holding a COO title, and exaggerating nonprofit accomplishments ([NewsChannel 5](https://www.newschannel5.com/news/newschannel-5-investigates/businessman-economist-cop-international-sex-crimes-expert-the-stories-of-congressman-andy-ogles); [MSNBC](https://www.msnbc.com/rachel-maddow-show/maddowblog/ethics-troubles-gops-andy-ogles-become-even-serious-rcna186362); [Tennessee Tribune](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:67ND-RNB1-JBHT-D1XK-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Ogles faced scrutiny over a GoFundMe charity campaign for a children’s burial garden, allegedly raising tens of thousands of dollars with no evidence the project was completed, and not accounting for the funds ([NewsChannel 5](https://www.newschannel5.com/news/newschannel-5-investigates/congressman-andy-ogles-faces-fec-complaint-over-numerous-discrepancies-in-campaign-reports); [MSNBC](https://www.msnbc.com/rachel-maddow-show/maddowblog/ethics-troubles-gops-andy-ogles-become-even-serious-rcna186362)).
