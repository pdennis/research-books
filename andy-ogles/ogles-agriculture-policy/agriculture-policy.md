# Agriculture Policy Positions and Initiatives

### Summary

> * Ogles supported legislation aimed at increasing transparency in agricultural markets, including backing reforms to the USDA’s Commodity Checkoff Programs and co-sponsoring the bipartisan Crop Insurance Transparency Act to require disclosure of federal crop insurance recipients ([Columbia Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CKD-GXK1-DXGX-62TV-00000-00&context=1519360), [Tennessean](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CKD-GXK1-DXGX-63P5-00000-00&context=1519360), [Leaf-Chronicle](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CKM-G1S1-JB1V-G0SP-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> 
> * He consistently opposed increases to reference prices in the Farm Bill, arguing that such measures would contribute to the national debt and inflation, and communicated this stance in a letter to House Agriculture Committee leadership ([Columbia Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CKD-GXK1-DXGX-62TV-00000-00&context=1519360), [Knoxville News-Sentinel](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CKD-H061-DXGX-61MM-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> 
> * Ogles has publicly advocated for the development of regional agricultural infrastructure, supporting proposals for a new agriculture center and efforts to bring the Tennessee State Fair to Columbia, believing such projects would generate local economic benefits ([Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5WD9-9HK1-DXVP-V2JP-00000-00&context=1519360), [Tennessean](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6301-GY31-DYJJ-P4GM-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> 
> * Advocacy groups such as Competitive Markets Action have endorsed and praised Ogles for being a strong proponent of market competitiveness and transparency in agricultural policy ([Columbia Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CKD-GXK1-DXGX-62TV-00000-00&context=1519360), [Leaf-Chronicle](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CKM-G1S1-JB1V-G0SP-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> 
> * Ogles has recognized local agricultural institutions like the Farm Bureau as vital to the community, citing shared values of hard work and their importance to Tennessee’s agricultural heritage ([Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6390-47M1-DXVP-V0RP-00000-00&context=1519360)).
