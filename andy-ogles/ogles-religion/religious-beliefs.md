# Personal Religious Beliefs and Affiliations

### Summary

> * Andy Ogles consistently frames his political beliefs and campaigns around a Christian worldview, emphasizing America's Christian roots and asserting that contemporary society has strayed from those foundations ([Jackson Sun](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66RS-47H1-JB1V-G0M7-00000-00&context=1519360), [Columbia Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66RS-47G1-JB1V-G4TV-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * He has publicly described current political and cultural conflicts as a "spiritual war," often blending religious language with political messaging ([Leaf-Chronicle](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66RS-47J1-DXGX-612D-00000-00&context=1519360), [Knoxville News-Sentinel](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66RS-47H1-JB1V-G12S-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Ogles has taken concrete actions tying government roles to religious practices, such as organizing prayer vigils following local tragedies and objecting to COVID-era restrictions on church gatherings ([Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5THF-7CF1-DXVP-V555-00000-00&context=1519360), [Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5YS1-P1G1-JBCN-404N-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * He has actively supported the use of religious exemptions in opposition to vaccine and mask mandates, offering assistance to health workers and families seeking such exemptions ([Columbia Daily Herald](https://www.columbiadailyherald.com/story/news/2021/11/18/ogles-issues-state-of-emergency-amid-mandatory-vaccination/8675443002/), [Jackson Sun](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66RS-47H1-JB1V-G0M7-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Critics and media have identified Ogles’ messaging as aligning with Christian nationalism, a label he has not directly addressed and on which he has declined interview requests ([Columbia Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66RS-47G1-JB1V-G4TV-00000-00&context=1519360), [Knoxville News-Sentinel](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66RS-47H1-JB1V-G12S-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Potential vulnerability: Ogles’ close alignment of faith and politics and perceived embrace of Christian nationalism could be polarizing or draw criticism in broader or more diverse constituencies.
