### Summary

> * Ogles publicly advocated overturning federal same-sex marriage protections, wanting states like Tennessee to outlaw gay marriage, a position likely to alienate moderate and LGBTQ+ voters ([Tennessee Lookout](https://tennesseelookout.com/2022/10/04/tennessees-new-5th-congressional-district-features-trump-republican-vs-progressive-democrat/)).
