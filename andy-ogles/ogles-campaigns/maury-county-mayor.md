# Maury County Mayoral Campaigns

### Summary

> * Andy Ogles was elected Maury County Mayor in 2018 after securing the Republican nomination through a GOP convention and defeating incumbent Charlie Norman, with strong backing from State Rep. Sheila Butt ([Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5T1C-SNT1-DXVP-V41G-00000-00&context=1519360), [Tennessean](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CMR-4961-DXGX-62PK-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Ogles ran on a platform focused on fiscal responsibility, tax transparency, responsible growth, and support for education, leveraging his background in business and policy while having no prior government experience ([Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5SM9-1DV1-JBCN-44S3-00000-00&context=1519360), [James Bennett - Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5TK6-CTW1-DXVP-V44Y-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * He and his campaign volunteers claimed extensive voter outreach, knocking on as many as 18,000 doors, which he credited for his electoral success ([Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5SYM-PC61-DXVP-V4WP-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * For the 2022 election, Ogles secured the GOP nomination for a second term as mayor unopposed but withdrew at the last minute to run for Congress, leaving the Republican Party unable to field another nominee and forcing other GOP candidates to run as independents ([Columbia Daily Herald](https://www.columbiadailyherald.com/story/news/2022/04/14/andy-ogles-maury-county-tennessee-mayor-exits-election-congress-district-5/7320332001/), [Tennessean](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:67NC-PNV1-DXGX-6421-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Ogles’ withdrawal from the mayoral race close to the deadline was seen as disruptive within the local Republican party, as it prevented them from holding a new caucus and selecting a replacement nominee ([Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:663N-CDN1-JBCN-43WY-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Throughout his career, Ogles has teased higher political ambitions, including potential runs for governor and Congress, regularly raising his profile through criticism of state leadership and active involvement at conservative events ([Tennessee Star](https://tennesseestar.com/news/maury-county-mayor-andy-ogles-conservative-tennessee-is-a-state-of-half-measures/klbaigert/2021/08/02/), [Knoxville News-Sentinel](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63KH-4031-DYJJ-P172-00000-00&context=1519360)).
