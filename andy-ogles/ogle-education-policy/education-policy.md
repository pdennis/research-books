# Legislative Actions and Campaign Positions on Education

### Summary

> * In 2024, Andy Ogles introduced legislation targeting student protesters, including the "Antisemitism Community Service Act" that would require those convicted of unlawful campus activities since October 7, 2023, to complete at least six months of community service in Gaza ([Knoxville News-Sentinel](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6C10-S181-JB1V-G31F-00000-00&context=1519360); [Tennessean](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6C10-S181-JB1V-G24V-00000-00&context=1519360); [Jackson Sun](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6C16-R8S1-DXGX-61PX-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Ogles also backed legislation to revoke visas for demonstrators who riot or engage in unlawful protests on university campuses ([Knoxville News-Sentinel](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6C10-S181-JB1V-G31F-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * During the COVID-19 pandemic, Ogles repeatedly called for reopening schools on time, citing the importance of in-person learning for students' mental health, social development, safety, and access to food ([Daily Herald, 5/28/20](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:601N-F641-JBCN-40N3-00000-00&context=1519360); [Daily Herald, 8/12/20](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:60K1-BK01-DXVP-V07N-00000-00&context=1519360); [Daily Herald, 7/13/20](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:60BV-63N1-JBCN-40R2-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Ogles emphasized the need for a unified approach to reopening plans and opposed extended school closures and ongoing executive orders over COVID-19, arguing it was politicized and not supported by data ([Daily Herald, 7/13/20](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:60BV-63N1-JBCN-40R2-00000-00&context=1519360); [Daily Herald, 9/8/20](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:60T3-14N1-DXVP-V2R6-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Ogles has publicly praised private schools and the county's improving education system, highlighting their role in fostering independent thinking and problem-solving in students ([Daily Herald, 8/6/19](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5WRP-1TP1-JBCN-41M5-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Potential vulnerability: Ogles' legislative focus on punishing student demonstrators, as well as the controversial provision to require convicted protesters to serve community service in Gaza, could invite legal scrutiny and public debate about constitutional rights and the efficacy of such measures.
