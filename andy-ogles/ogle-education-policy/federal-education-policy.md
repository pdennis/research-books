# Federal Role and Education Policy

### Summary

> * Andy Ogles has consistently called for eliminating or defunding the U.S. Department of Education, favoring local and state control over education policy ([Tennessean](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66R5-4KS1-JB1V-G4YC-00000-00&context=1519360), [Columbia Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D0P-YKB1-DXGX-617C-00000-00&context=1519360), [Leaf-Chronicle](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66XP-HWP1-DXGX-6263-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * He has frequently criticized federal involvement as promoting "woke principles" and undermining basic educational skills, arguing that Tennessee should have autonomy in setting curricula and standards ([Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:65TB-GVM1-DXVP-V4NM-00000-00&context=1519360), [Tennessean](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66P3-8V51-JB1V-G38G-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Ogles advocates for parental rights and local decision-making, opposing state or federal oversight in issues such as curriculum content, COVID-19 response, and student well-being checks ([Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:60KW-74N1-DXVP-V058-00000-00&context=1519360), [Tennessee Star](https://tennesseestar.com/news/maury-county-mayor-andy-ogles-conservative-tennessee-is-a-state-of-half-measures/klbaigert/2021/08/02/)).
> * He has criticized both state and federal education officials, including calls for the removal of Tennessee’s education commissioner, and claims that current leadership does not reflect local values ([Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:60V4-VS61-JBCN-4457-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Ogles’s hardline stance on federal education policy could be seen as a vulnerability, as critics argue that abolishing the Department of Education may jeopardize federal funding and civil protections for disadvantaged and disabled students ([Tennessean](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D0X-XR91-JB1V-G4YT-00000-00&context=1519360), [Columbia Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D0P-YKB1-DXGX-617C-00000-00&context=1519360)).
