### Public Health and School Operations

#### <span style="text-decoration: underline; border-bottom: 1px solid #999; padding-bottom: 2px;">Opposition to COVID-19 Mandates in Schools</span>

**2020: Andy Ogles Stated Need To Reopen Schools In Maury County** According to Daily Herald, 'I am frustrated. We need to get our kids back in schools and we need to get them educated,' Ogles said. 'Those most at risk are paying double, triple the price. It is unacceptable. We need to educate our kids.' [Daily Herald, [9/11/20](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:60V4-VS61-JBCN-444H-00000-00&context=1519360)]

**September 2020: Andy Ogles Urged Consistency In School Opening Policies During COVID-19** According to The Daily Herald, "Thursday, Ogles continued to reiterate his opinion that the state's schools should continue to remain open amid the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic. 'We have got to figure out how to keep our schools open,' Ogles said during his daily update recorded and shared on social media. 'I am not saying that it is going to be easy. The constant opening and closing of schools has a negative impact, and I really implore our department of education to help our districts and have a more consistent policy across the state.'" [Daily Herald (Columbia, Tennessee), [9/13/20](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:60V4-VS61-JBCN-4457-00000-00&context=1519360)]
