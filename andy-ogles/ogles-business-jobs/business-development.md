# Business, Employment and Economic Development

### Summary

> * Andy Ogles prioritized keeping Maury County businesses open during the COVID-19 pandemic, emphasizing the need to support the local economy and downtown square ([Daily Herald](https://www.columbiadailyherald.com/story/news/local/2021/10/04/finding-balance-growth-defines-ogles-role-leading-maury-county/5934788001/)).
> * Ogles played a key role in negotiating major economic investments, including a $4.3 billion GM plant expansion and other projects that brought more than 1,200 new jobs to the region ([Tennessean](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6301-GY31-DYJJ-P4GM-00000-00&context=1519360), [Tennessean](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63V4-6SX1-JC3H-0522-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Negotiations for the GM investment and associated payment in lieu of taxes (PILOT) program lasted nearly five months and were described as ensuring the facility's future in Maury County for another 20 years, while also increasing tax revenue ([Tennessean](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6147-31P1-DYJJ-P2P0-00000-00&context=1519360), [Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6146-F661-JBCN-44VP-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Maury County has experienced rapid economic growth, with rankings as the top county in Tennessee for incoming investments and significant increases in new businesses, job creation, and residential housing ([Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63PG-JTH1-JBCN-42N8-00000-00&context=1519360), [Tennessean](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63V4-6SX1-JC3H-0542-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Ogles frequently described Maury County as business-friendly with a low cost of living, positioning it as a "must-see destination" for residents and investors ([Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63PG-JTH1-JBCN-42N8-00000-00&context=1519360), [Tennessean](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63V4-6SX1-JC3H-0542-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Potential vulnerabilities may exist in the county's reliance on large economic deals and ongoing negotiations, which could pose financial or operational risks if future investments do not materialize as planned.
