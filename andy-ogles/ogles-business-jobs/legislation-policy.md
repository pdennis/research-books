# Legislative Actions and Policy Stances

### Summary

> * Andy Ogles consistently opposed increases to Tennessee's fuel and gas taxes in 2017, lobbying against the measures but was ultimately unsuccessful ([Tennessee Lookout](https://tennesseelookout.com/2022/07/26/ogles-late-fundraising-report-shows-less-than-touted-in-5th-congressional-district-race/), [Commercial Appeal](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5NTT-VPW1-JBCN-42M4-00000-00&context=1519360), [Tennessean](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5NV2-5FJ1-DYJJ-P00M-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * In 2020, Ogles advocated for increasing Maury County's sales tax to align with neighboring counties, citing the need to meet the demands of a growing community ([Daily Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:61N4-J8H1-DXVP-V0GK-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Ogles consistently promoted Tennessee's low-tax environment and right-to-work laws as key factors for business investment and economic reputation, highlighting the state's appeal to major corporations ([Leaf-Chronicle](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63XP-BNH1-JC3H-022K-00000-00&context=1519360), [Tennessean](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63V4-6SX1-JC3H-0522-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * During the COVID-19 pandemic, Ogles strongly pushed for early reopening of the local economy, opposition to restrictions, and support for keeping businesses in Maury County open, often criticizing executive orders for harming the economy ([Daily Herald 1](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5YPX-XXC1-DXVP-V13G-00000-00&context=1519360), [Daily Herald 2](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:601N-F641-JBCN-40N3-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Ogles emphasized the strong work ethic and reputation of Tennessee workers as a selling point for attracting investment, asserting that the state offered a high return on investment for companies ([Leaf-Chronicle](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63XP-BNH1-JC3H-022K-00000-00&context=1519360), [Tennessean](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63V4-6SX1-JC3H-0522-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Potential vulnerability: Ogles' unsuccessful efforts to block the fuel/gas tax increase in 2017 could be seen as a legislative defeat or lack of effectiveness in shaping state tax policy ([Tennessee Lookout](https://tennesseelookout.com/2022/07/26/ogles-late-fundraising-report-shows-less-than-touted-in-5th-congressional-district-race/)).
