# Immediate Family and Personal Background

### Summary

-   Jeff Hurd is married to Barbora Hurd, a naturalized citizen originally from Czechoslovakia who immigrated after growing up under communist rule---an experience that both he and his campaign say has shaped his appreciation for American freedoms ([CPR News](https://www.cpr.org/2024/06/03/vg-2024-colorado-primary-elections-congressional-district-3-candidate-jeff-hurd/), [Colorado Politics](https://www.coloradopolitics.com/elections/jeff-hurd-digital-ad-lauren-boebert-congress-district-3/article_3454ea26-6c50-11ee-81bb-13bfdef5b1da.html)).

-   The Hurds have five children---three boys and two girls---and have lived in Grand Junction, Colorado, where family and Christian values are central to their lives ([JeffHurdForColorado.com](https://jeffhurdforcolorado.com/meet-jeff/), [Denver Post](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6902-6X71-DXVP-T3XR-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Barbora Hurd has publicly endorsed Jeff's integrity and work ethic, calling him a devoted husband and father and highlighting his commitment to family values in campaign materials ([Colorado Politics](https://www.coloradopolitics.com/elections/jeff-hurd-digital-ad-lauren-boebert-congress-district-3/article_3454ea26-6c50-11ee-81bb-13bfdef5b1da.html), [Coloradopolitics.com](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:69DN-G8X1-DXVP-T4DF-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Jeff Hurd emphasizes spending time in Colorado with his family during Congressional district work weeks, underscoring his prioritization of family amidst his public service ([The Daily Sentinel](https://www.gjsentinel.com/news/western_colorado/q-a-u-s-house-rep-jeff-hurd-talks-early-days-in-congress/article_e380af5e-e25f-11ef-a710-0bd3c4a60687.html)).

-   Community leaders and opponents have characterized Hurd as a strong family man, with bipartisan acknowledgment of his dedication as a husband and father ([5280 Magazine](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D3H-85K1-DXVP-T4DD-00000-00&context=1519360), [Denver Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6C92-1J71-JBCN-30S9-00000-00&context=1519360)).
