# Agricultural Policy

### Summary

-   Jeff Hurd has prioritized water rights and infrastructure, strongly opposing water diversion projects that would move resources from Western Slope and the San Luis Valley to other regions, including the Front Range and metro Denver ([Durango Herald](https://www.durangoherald.com/articles/3rd-congressional-district-candidate-jeff-hurd-visits-bayfield/); [Denver Post](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D60-KM21-DXVP-T548-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   He supports lowering food and production costs by expanding domestic energy production, removing unnecessary agricultural regulations, and strengthening local supply chains ([CPR News](https://www.cpr.org/2024/10/14/vg-2024-3rd-congressional-district-jeff-hurd/)).

-   Hurd has advocated for fair federal land and forest management policies, including reforming the PILT formula, supporting the Secure Rural Schools program and the Farm Bill, and serving as an original sponsor of the bipartisan Fix Our Forests Act ([Durango Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6F6N-MJN3-RS1T-153J-00000-00&context=1519360); [The Journal](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6FMS-GJT3-RSMT-0323-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   He emphasizes protecting local interests in water negotiations, calling for stronger advocacy against the outsized influence of downstream states over Colorado River water use and compliance from states like California, Nevada, and Arizona ([Denver Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D21-FMW1-DXVP-T4WT-00000-00&context=1519360); [Journal](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6BX1-1031-DXVP-T459-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Potential vulnerabilities include opposition attacks alleging ties to controversial water transfer plans, though Hurd has firmly denied any support for such projects and advocated for local input in water decisions ([Coloradopolitics.com](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CXS-X961-DXVP-T2W4-00000-00&context=1519360); [Denver Post](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D60-KM21-DXVP-T548-00000-00&context=1519360)).
