# Legal Career and Professional Experience

### Summary

-   Jeff Hurd built a successful legal career in Grand Junction, Colorado, specializing in commercial and regulatory law and leading the local office of Ireland Stapleton Pryor & Pascoe PC, a major state law firm; he focused on representing rural electric cooperatives, counties, school and fire districts, water districts, and broadband providers ([JeffHurdForColorado.com](https://jeffhurdforcolorado.com/meet-jeff/), [Durango Herald](https://www.durangoherald.com/articles/3rd-congressional-district-candidate-jeff-hurd-visits-bayfield/), [Denver Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DJR-KWT1-DXVP-T3BY-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   He previously served as chief counsel for the Delta-Montrose Electric Association, notably working on legal and negotiation issues related to broadband and energy for rural communities ([Fort Morgan Times](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6FM2-M0M3-RWPF-44M0-00000-00&context=1519360), [Durango Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6FFP-H063-RS61-22B3-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Hurd's professional leadership extended to business organizations, serving as board chairman of the Grand Junction Area Chamber of Commerce and actively engaging with the broader legal and business communities ([Denver Post](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6902-6X71-DXVP-T3XR-00000-00&context=1519360), [Montrose Daily Press](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CBS-3KC1-JBCN-31C1-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   He contributed to state legal policy by co-authoring a significant 2018 legal brief supporting Colorado Amendment 71, and has served on federal and state judicial committees ([JeffHurdForColorado.com](https://jeffhurdforcolorado.com/meet-jeff/), [Denver Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DJR-KWT1-DXVP-T3BY-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Hurd's legal experience was frequently cited as both an asset and a vulnerability during his political campaigns, with opponents describing him as a "corporate lawyer" and highlighting his work for business interests ([Colorado Sun](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D13-8GS1-JBCN-33B8-00000-00&context=1519360), [Durango Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D9P-5MV1-JBCN-340K-00000-00&context=1519360)).
