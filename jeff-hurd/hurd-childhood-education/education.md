# Education

### Summary

-   Jeff Hurd graduated from Grand Junction High School before attending the University of Notre Dame, where he worked various jobs, including washing dishes, to help pay for college ([JeffHurdForColorado.com](https://jeffhurdforcolorado.com/meet-jeff/), [Montrose Daily Press](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:68YM-DTX1-DXVP-T442-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   He earned his law degree from the University of Denver Sturm College of Law, graduating with high honors ([JeffHurdForColorado.com](https://jeffhurdforcolorado.com/meet-jeff/)).

-   Hurd further obtained an advanced law degree (LLM) from Columbia University School of Law, again graduating with high honors ([JeffHurdForColorado.com](https://jeffhurdforcolorado.com/meet-jeff/), [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jeff_Hurd_(politician))).

-   After law school, he clerked for federal appeals court judge Timothy M. Tymkovich in Denver ([JeffHurdForColorado.com](https://jeffhurdforcolorado.com/meet-jeff/), [Coloradopolitics.com](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:68YW-7CB1-JBCN-30XS-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Hurd's educational background has consistently been highlighted in media coverage, reflecting both academic achievement and perseverance through self-support during college ([Montrose Daily Press](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CBS-3KC1-JBCN-31C1-00000-00&context=1519360)).
