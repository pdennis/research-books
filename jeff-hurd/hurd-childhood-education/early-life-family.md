# Early Life and Family Background

### Summary

-   Jeff Hurd was born in Grand Junction, Colorado in 1979 and raised there, attending local public schools and participating in community activities like Little League and public swimming ([JeffHurdForColorado.com](https://jeffhurdforcolorado.com/meet-jeff/), [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jeff_Hurd_(politician)), [Newsweek](https://www.newsweek.com/lauren-boebert-challenger-jeff-hurd-celebrity-status-1836938)).

-   He identifies as a lifelong resident of Colorado's 3rd Congressional District, with deep roots on the Western Slope and involvement in the local community ([Burlington Record](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CBJ-Y4C1-DXVP-T04K-00000-00&context=1519360), [Denver Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D21-FMW1-DXVP-T4WT-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Hurd's mother passed away from cancer while he was in high school, a loss that deeply affected his family and shaped his values ([JeffHurdForColorado.com](https://jeffhurdforcolorado.com/meet-jeff/), [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jeff_Hurd_(politician))).

-   His father worked as a psychologist counseling low-income families, contributing to Hurd's upbringing and perspective on community service ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jeff_Hurd_(politician))).

-   After attending college and practicing law in New York, Hurd returned to Grand Junction in 2014 to raise his family and engage in local business and civic roles ([Newsweek](https://www.newsweek.com/lauren-boebert-challenger-jeff-hurd-celebrity-status-1836938), [Montrose Daily Press](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:695M-XPD1-DXVP-T200-00000-00&context=1519360)).
