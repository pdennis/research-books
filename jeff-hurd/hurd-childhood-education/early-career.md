# Early Career Foundations

### Summary

-   Jeff Hurd began his legal career at Sullivan & Cromwell, a prestigious international law firm in New York City ([JeffHurdForColorado.com](https://jeffhurdforcolorado.com/meet-jeff/)).

-   After several years in New York, Hurd decided to return to Western Colorado to be closer to home ([JeffHurdForColorado.com](https://jeffhurdforcolorado.com/meet-jeff/)).

-   In 2014, Hurd and his wife Barbora moved their family back to Grand Junction ([JeffHurdForColorado.com](https://jeffhurdforcolorado.com/meet-jeff/)).

-   Upon returning to Grand Junction, Hurd became co-owner of a small law firm ([JeffHurdForColorado.com](https://jeffhurdforcolorado.com/meet-jeff/)).

-   Hurd's move highlights a shift from corporate law in a large city to a focus on community and family life in Western Colorado ([JeffHurdForColorado.com](https://jeffhurdforcolorado.com/)).
