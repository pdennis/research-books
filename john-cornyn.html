Political Vulnerabilities

Cornyn Voted To Benefit Large Corporations And Hurt The Middle Class With The Trump Tax Cuts

  • Cornyn wanted to renew the Tax Cuts and Jobs Act, Trump’s 2017 tax cuts.
  • Cornyn secured initial passage and fended off revisions for Trump’s tax cuts.
  • Cornyn inserted a provision in the Trump tax bill’s negotiations that benefited pass-through businesses, special interests, and the wealthiest Americans.

Cornyn Was Bad For Women And Sought To Restrict Abortion Rights

  • Cornyn was top rated by anti-choice organizations and celebrated the Dobbs decision.
  • Cornyn voted to confirm anti-choice judges.
  • Cornyn voted for federal 20-week abortion bans.
  • Cornyn voted to restrict access to private insurance plans that offered abortion coverage.

Cornyn Repeatedly Failed To Protect Social Security Benefits

  • Cornyn defended Elon Musk and DOGE’s cuts to the Social Security Administration.
  • Cornyn voted to facilitate raising the retirement age.
  • Cornyn voted against expanding and protecting social security benefits.
  • Cornyn voted to enable the privatization of social security.

Cornyn Opposed Medicaid Expansions And Backed Conservative Medicaid Reforms

  • Cornyn called for a Medicaid work requirement and proposed replacing the program with block grants..
  • Cornyn voted to facilitate a decrease in Medicaid enrollment in expansion states.
  • Cornyn repeatedly voted against Medicaid expansions.

Cornyn Backed Medicare Cuts And Privatization

  • Cornyn voted repeatedly for GOP budgets that cut Medicare by nearly $500 billion.
  • Cornyn voted to facilitate Medicare benefits cuts and privatization.
  • Cornyn voted to raise the Medicare eligibility age.

Cornyn Was A Key Player In The GOP’s Frenzy To Repeal The Affordable Care Act

  • Prior to and throughout 2017, Cornyn attempted to repeal the Affordable Care Act on numerous occasions.
  • Once Trump became President, the San Antonio News Express described Cornyn as playing an “instrumental role” in the efforts to repeal the Affordable Care Act.
  • Cornyn, on numerous occasions, made it clear that he desired to repeal the Affordable Care Act. 

Cornyn Was A DC Insider And Creature Of The Swamp

  • Cornyn was Mitch McConnell’s “lapdog” and was closely aligned with the former GOP Leader.
  • Cornyn called for a freeze on congressional pay during a government shutdown but still accepted his salary. 
  • Cornyn supported measures to raise his own salary, which had increased by $24,000 during his time in the Senate.
