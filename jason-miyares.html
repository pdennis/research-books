Political Vulnerabilities

As Attorney General and a member of the Virginia state legislature, Jason Miyares posed a threat to Virginia’s state of abortion rights, election integrity, education, LGBTQ rights, and health care. 

Anti-Abortion Extremist

Miyares supported anti-abortion pregnancy centers and the overturning of Roe V. Wade, and spread false claims about pro-choice legislation.

Threat To Democracy

Miyares supported legislation that disenfranchised voters including requiring proof of citizenship and purging voter rolls and established an “Election Integrity Unit.” 

Supported Defunding Public Schools

Miyares supported charter schools, voted to defund public schools, and opposed protections for LGBTQ students and teaching Critical Race Theory.

Anti-Equality Agenda

Miyares voted against legislation that banned conversion therapy and removed prohibitions on same-sex marriage. 

Opposed Medicaid Expansion

Miyares voted against Medicaid expansion that now covers 400,000 Virginians.
