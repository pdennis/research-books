# Natural Resources Conservation Service

### Funding

**2022: Fitzpatrick Voted For An Amendment That Reallocated \$2 Million
To The Natural Resources Conservation Service For Conservation
Operations And Technical Help For Farmers.** In July 2022, according to
Congressional Quarterly, Fitzpatrick voted for en bloc amendments no. 2
to the Transportation, Housing and Urban Development, and Related
Agencies Appropriations Act, 2023, which would, in part, "reallocate \$2
million to the Natural Resources Conservation Service for conservation
operations and technical assistance for farmers." The vote was on the
adoption of amendments. The House adopted the amendments by a vote
336-90. \[House Vote 368,
[7/19/22](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2022/roll368.xml); Congressional
Quarterly, [7/19/22](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2022/H/368?2);
Congressional Actions, [H.Amdt.
294](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/117th-congress/house-amendment/294/actions?s=a&r=8);
Congressional Actions, [H.R.
8294](https://www.congress.gov/bill/117th-congress/house-bill/8294/actions)\]
