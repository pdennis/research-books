# Speaker Of The House

### Election

**2021: Fitzpatrick Voted For \[Representative Nancy
Pelosi/Representative Kevin McCarthy\] To Be Speaker Of The House For
The 117th Congress.** In January 2021, according to Congressional
Quarterly, Fitzpatrick voted for \[Nancy Pelosi/Kevin McCarthy\] in the
"nomination of Nancy Pelosi, D-Calif., and Kevin McCarthy, R-Calif., for
Speaker of House of Representatives for the 117th Congress." The vote
was on the election of the speaker. The House elected Nancy Pelosi as
Speaker of the House by a vote of 216-209. \[House Vote 2,
[1/3/21](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2021/roll002.xml); Congressional
Quarterly, [1/3/21](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2021/H/2?10)\]

**2021: Fitzpatrick Voted To Elect Rep. Kevin McCarthy To Be Speaker Of
The House For The 117th Congress.** In January 2021, Fitzpatrick voted
to elect Rep. Kevin McCarthy (R-CA) as Speaker of the House for the
117th Congress. The vote was on the election of the speaker. The House
elected Rep. Nancy Pelosi (D-CA) as Speaker of the House by a vote of
216-209. \[House Vote 2,
[1/3/21](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2021/roll002.xml)\]
