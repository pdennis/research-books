# Suicide

### VA Suicide Prevention Coordinators

**2022: Fitzpatrick Voted For An Amendment That Reallocated \$1 Million
For VA Suicide Prevention Coordinators.** In July 2022, according to
Congressional Quarterly, Fitzpatrick voted for en bloc amendments no. 7
to the Transportation, Housing and Urban Development, and Related
Agencies Appropriations Act, 2023, which would, in part, "reallocate \$1
million for VA medical services, intended to support VA suicide
prevention coordinators." The vote was on the adoption of amendments.
The House adopted the amendments by a vote 355-56. \[House Vote 379,
[7/20/22](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2022/roll379.xml); Congressional
Quarterly, [7/20/22](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2022/H/379?0);
Congressional Actions, [H.Amdt.
299](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/117th-congress/house-amendment/299/actions?s=a&r=3);
Congressional Actions, [H.R.
8294](https://www.congress.gov/bill/117th-congress/house-bill/8294/actions)\]
