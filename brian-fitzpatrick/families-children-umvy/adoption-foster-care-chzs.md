# Adoption And Foster Care

### Funding

**2021: Fitzpatrick Voted To Authorize \$40 Million For FY 2022 And As
Necessary Through FY 2027 To Aid Adoption And Foster Care Programs.** In
March 2021, Fitzpatrick voted for the Stronger Child Abuse Prevention
and Treatment Act which would, according to Congressional Quarterly,
"authorize \$40 million for fiscal 2022 and such sums as may be
necessary through fiscal 2027 for HHS activities to support adoption and
foster care programs." The vote was on passage. The House passed the
bill by a vote of 345-73. The House passed the bill by a vote of 345-73.
The Senate did not take substantive action on the bill. \[House Vote 81,
[3/16/21](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2021/roll081.xml); Congressional
Quarterly, [3/16/21](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2021/H/81?10);
Congressional Actions, [H.R.
485](https://www.congress.gov/bill/117th-congress/house-bill/485/actions)\]
