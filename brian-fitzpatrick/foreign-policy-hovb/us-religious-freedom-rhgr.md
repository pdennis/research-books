# U.S. Commission On International Religious Freedom

### Funding

**2022: Fitzpatrick Voted To Provide \$3.5 Million Annually For FY
2023-2024 For The Reauthorization Of The U.S. Commission On
International Religious Freedom.** In September 2022, according to
Congressional Quarterly, Fitzpatrick voted for the United States
Commission on International Religious Freedom Reauthorization Act of
2022, which would "reauthorize the U.S. Commission on International
Religious Freedom, authorizing \$3.5 million annually for fiscal years
2023 through 2024." The vote was on passage. The House passed the bill
by a vote of 402-4, thus the bill was sent to President Biden and it
ultimately became law. \[House Vote 438,
[9/19/22](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2022/roll438.xml); Congressional
Quarterly, [9/19/22](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2022/H/438?15);
Congressional Actions, [S.
3895](https://www.congress.gov/bill/117th-congress/senate-bill/3895/all-actions)\]
