# Lebanon

### Military Aid

**2024: Fitzpatrick Voted Against Prohibiting Aid To The Lebanese Armed
Forces.** In June 2024, Fitzpatrick voted against , according to
Congressional Quarterly, "amendment no. 61 that would prohibit the use
of funds provided by the bill for the Lebanese armed forces." The vote
was on the amendment. The underlying legislation was the FY 2025
State-Foreign Operations Appropriations. The House rejected the
amendment by a vote of 103 to 308. \[House Vote 317,
[6/27/24](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2024/roll317.xml); Congressional
Quarterly, [6/27/24](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2024/H/317?11);
Congressional Actions,
[H.Amdt.1059](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/118th-congress/house-amendment/1059/actions);
Congressional Actions, [H.R.
8771](http://www.congress.gov/bill/118th-congress/house-bill/8771/all-actions)\]
