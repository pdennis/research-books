# Extending Use Of Coronavirus Relief Funds

**2021: Fitzpatrick Voted For Extending Through December 31, 2022 The
Use Of Coronavirus Relief Funds From The CARES Act Of 2020 To Tribal
Governments.** In December 2021, Fitzpatrick voted for the bill which
would, according to Congressional Quarterly, "authorize tribal
governments to use coronavirus relief funds provided by prior law (PL
116-136) to cover costs incurred through Dec. 31, 2022 \-- an extension
of one year." The vote was on passage. The House passed the bill by a
vote of 386-41. \[House Vote 421,
[12/8/21](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2021/roll421.xml); Congressional
Quarterly, [12/8/21](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2021/H/421?8);
Congressional Actions, [H.R.
5119](https://www.congress.gov/bill/117th-congress/house-bill/5119/actions)\]
