# COVID-19 Testing

### Maintaining Testing Rule For Travelers From China

**2023: Fitzpatrick Voted For An Amendment That Would Specify That
Ending The CDC Foreign Traveler COVID-19 Vaccine Rule Would Not Affect
The CDC's Rule Requiring A Negative COVID-19 Test Result Or Recovery
Documentation For Travelers From China.** In February 2023, according to
Congressional Quarterly, Fitzpatrick voted for an amendment to a bill
that would "specify that the bill\'s provisions would not affect a
January 2023 Centers for Disease Control and Prevention order requiring
a negative pre-departure COVID-19 test result or documentation of
recovery from COVID-19 for aircraft passengers who are traveling to the
United States from China or have been in China within the 10 days prior
to U.S. entry." The vote was on the adoption the amendment. The House
adopted the amendment by a vote of 426 to 8. \[House Vote 113,
[2/8/23](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2023/roll113.xml); Congressional
Quarterly, [2/8/23](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2023/H/113?18);

83](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/118th-congress/house-amendment/83/actions?s=a&r=2);
Congressional Actions, [H.R.
185](https://www.congress.gov/bill/118th-congress/house-bill/185/all-actions)\]
