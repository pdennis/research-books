# Essential Air Service

### Rescinding Authorization And Funding

**2023: Fitzpatrick Voted Against An Amendment That Would Have Removed A
\$1.5 Billion Appropriation For The Essential Air Service Over 5
Years.** In July 2023, according to Congressional Quarterly, Fitzpatrick
voted against an amendment to the Securing Growth and Robust Leadership
in American Aviation Act, which would "strike language that would
authorize \$1.5 billion over five years for the Essential Air Service."
The vote was on the adoption of an amendment. The House rejected the
amendment by a vote of 49 to 386. \[House Vote 351,
[7/19/23](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2023/roll351.xml); Congressional
Quarterly, [7/19/23](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2023/H/351?3);
Congressional Actions, [H.R.
3935](https://www.congress.gov/bill/118th-congress/house-bill/3935/all-actions);
Congressional Actions, [H.Amdt.
276](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/118th-congress/house-amendment/276/actions?s=a&r=13)\]
