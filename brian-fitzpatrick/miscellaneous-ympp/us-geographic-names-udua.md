# U.S. Board On Geographic Names

### Funding

**2024: Fitzpatrick Voted Against Prohibiting The Use Of
Interior-Environment Funding For The U.S. Board On Geographic Names.**
In July 2024, Fitzpatrick voted against , according to Congressional
Quarterly, "amendment no. 45 that would prohibit the use of funds
provided by the bill for the U.S. Board on Geographic Names." The vote
was on the amendment. The underlying legislation was the FY 2025
Interior-Environment appropriations. The House rejected the amendment by
a vote of 174 to 239. \[House Vote 389,
[7/24/24](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2024/roll389.xml); Congressional
Quarterly, [7/24/24](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2024/H/389?3);
Congressional Actions,
[H.Amdt.1152](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/118th-congress/house-amendment/1152/actions);
Congressional Actions, [H.R.
8998](https://www.congress.gov/bill/118th-congress/house-bill/8998/all-actions)\]
