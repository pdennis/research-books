# Woodrow Wilson International Center For Scholars

### Funding

**2024: Fitzpatrick Voted Against Reducing Funding For The Woodrow
Wilson International Center For Scholars By \$12 Million.** In July
2024, Fitzpatrick voted against , according to Congressional Quarterly,
"amendment no. 24 that would reduce by \$12 million funding for the
Woodrow Wilson International Center for Scholars and transfer the same
amount to the spending reduction account." The vote was on the
amendment. The underlying legislation was the FY 2025
Interior-Environment appropriations. The House rejected the amendment by
a vote of 156 to 236. \[House Vote 387,
[7/24/24](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2024/roll387.xml); Congressional
Quarterly, [7/24/24](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2024/H/387?3);
Congressional Actions,
[H.Amdt.1144](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/118th-congress/house-amendment/1144/actions);
Congressional Actions, [H.R.
8998](https://www.congress.gov/bill/118th-congress/house-bill/8998/all-actions)\]
