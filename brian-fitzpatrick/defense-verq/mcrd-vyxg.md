# Marine Corps Recruit Depot

### Closure

**2024: Fitzpatrick Voted To Prohibit The Closure Of The Marine Corps
Recruit Depot In Parris Island, South Carolina.** In June 2024,
Fitzpatrick voted for , according to Congressional Quarterly, "amendment
no. 28 that would prohibit the use of funds authorized or made available
to the Defense Department for any fiscal year for closure or realignment
of Marine Corps Recruit Depot, Parris Island, S.C." The vote was on the
amendment. It was rejected by a vote of 201 to 217. \[House Vote 257,
[6/12/24](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2024/roll257.xml); Congressional
Quarterly, [6/12/24](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2024/H/257?11);
Congressional Actions,
[H.Amdt.969](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/118th-congress/house-amendment/969/actions?s=a&r=34);
Congressional Actions, [H.R.
8070](http://www.congress.gov/bill/118th-congress/house-bill/8070/all-actions)\]
