# Department Of Defense Education Activity

### "Radical Gender Ideology"

**2024: Fitzpatrick Voted To Prohibit Military Schools Activity From
Displaying Materials In Classrooms That Promote "Radical Gender
Ideology."** In June 2024, Fitzpatrick voted for , according to
Congressional Quarterly, "amendment no. 46 that would prohibit funds
authorized or made available in fiscal 2025 or any fiscal year
thereafter under the bill for the Defense Department Education Activity
to purchase, display or maintain material in a school or classroom that
promotes radical gender ideology or pornographic content." The vote was
on the amendment. The underlying legislation was the FY 2025 National
Defense Authorization Act. The House adopted the amendment by a vote of
221 to 202. \[House Vote 271,
[6/13/24](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2024/roll271.xml); Congressional
Quarterly, [6/13/24](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2024/H/271?11);
Congressional Actions,
[H.Amdt.980](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/118th-congress/house-amendment/980/actions?s=a&r=34);
Congressional Actions, [H.R.
8070](http://www.congress.gov/bill/118th-congress/house-bill/8070/all-actions)\]
