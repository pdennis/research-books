# Delta Regional Commission

### Funding

**2024: Fitzpatrick Voted Against Reducing Funding For The Delta
Regional Authority.** In July 2024, Fitzpatrick voted against ,
according to Congressional Quarterly, "amendment no. 46 that would
reduce funding for the Delta Regional Authority by \$7.1 million and
transfer the savings to the Spending Reduction Account." The vote was on
the amendment. The underlying legislation was the FY 2025 Energy-Water
appropriations. The House rejected the amendment by a vote of 121 to
298. \[House Vote 368,
[7/23/24](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2024/roll368.xml); Congressional
Quarterly, [7/23/24](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2024/H/368?3);
Congressional Actions,
[H.Amdt.1108](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/118th-congress/house-amendment/1108/actions);
Congressional Actions, [H.R.
8997](https://www.congress.gov/bill/118th-congress/house-bill/8997/all-actions)\]
