# Southeast Crescent Regional Commission

### Funding

**2024: Fitzpatrick Voted Against Reducing Funding For The Southeast
Crescent Regional Commission.** In July 2024, Fitzpatrick voted against
, according to Congressional Quarterly, "amendment no. 48 that would
reduce funding by \$19.8 million for the Southeast Crescent Regional
Commission and transfer the savings to the Spending Reduction Account."
The vote was on the amendment. The underlying legislation was the FY
2025 Energy-Water appropriations. The House rejected the amendment by a
vote of 128 to 291. \[House Vote 370,
[7/23/24](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2024/roll370.xml); Congressional
Quarterly, [7/23/24](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2024/H/370?3);
Congressional Actions,
[H.Amdt.1110](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/118th-congress/house-amendment/1110/actions);
Congressional Actions, [H.R.
8997](https://www.congress.gov/bill/118th-congress/house-bill/8997/all-actions)\]
