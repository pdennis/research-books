# Great Lakes Region

### Great Lakes Authority

**2024: Fitzpatrick Voted Against Reducing Funding For The Great Lakes
Authority.** In July 2024, Fitzpatrick voted against , according to
Congressional Quarterly, "amendment no. 50 that would reduce funding for
the Great Lakes Authority by \$2.5 million and transfer the savings to
the Spending Reduction Account." The vote was on the amendment. The
underlying legislation was the FY 2025 Energy-Water appropriations. The
House rejected the amendment by a vote of 125 to 295. \[House Vote 372,
[7/23/24](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2024/roll372.xml); Congressional
Quarterly, [7/23/24](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2024/H/372?3);
Congressional Actions,
[H.Amdt.1112](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/118th-congress/house-amendment/1112/actions);
Congressional Actions, [H.R.
8997](https://www.congress.gov/bill/118th-congress/house-bill/8997/all-actions)\]
