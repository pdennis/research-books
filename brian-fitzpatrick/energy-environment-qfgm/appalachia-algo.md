# Appalachia

### Appalachian Regional Commission

**2024: Fitzpatrick Voted Against Reducing Funding For The Appalachian
Regional Commission.** In July 2024, Fitzpatrick voted against ,
according to Congressional Quarterly, "amendment no. 45 that would
reduce funding for the Appalachian Regional Commission by \$35 million
and transfer the savings to the Spending Reduction Account." The vote
was on the amendment. The underlying legislation was the FY 2025
Energy-Water appropriations. The House rejected the amendment by a vote
of 115 to 305. \[House Vote 367,
[7/23/24](http://clerk.house.gov/evs/2024/roll367.xml); Congressional
Quarterly, [7/23/24](https://plus.cq.com/vote/2024/H/367?3);
Congressional Actions,
[H.Amdt.1107](https://www.congress.gov/amendment/118th-congress/house-amendment/1107/actions);
Congressional Actions, [H.R.
8997](https://www.congress.gov/bill/118th-congress/house-bill/8997/all-actions)\]
