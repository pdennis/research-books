# Legislative Record on LGBTQ Rights

### Summary

-   In February 2021, Maria Elvira Salazar voted against the Equality Act, which sought to prohibit discrimination based on gender identity and sexual orientation by amending the Civil Rights and Fair Housing Acts ([Miami Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6396-RRD1-DYJM-M4TC-00000-00&context=1519360), [El Nuevo Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6397-D1W1-DYJM-M02S-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Salazar initially voted in favor of the Respect for Marriage Act in July 2022, which aimed to protect same-sex marriage rights at the federal level ([South Florida Sun-Sentinel](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:662C-1XF1-JBM5-R1PS-00000-00&context=1519360), [Orlando Sentinel](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:662C-HN71-DY37-F224-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   In December 2022, Salazar switched her vote and opposed the final passage of the Respect for Marriage Act, citing concerns over religious protections not being included in the final version ([South Florida Sun-Sentinel](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6723-X2D1-DY37-F1RK-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   This vote switch drew criticism from supporters of LGBTQ rights and some media, who accused Salazar of inconsistency and questioned her commitment to protecting marriage equality ([Miami Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6724-JGF1-DYJM-M2F3-00000-00&context=1519360), [El Nuevo Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:672T-4F41-JC3J-X2X1-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Salazar's record indicates a mixed approach to LGBTQ rights legislation, with votes both for and against significant federal protections, opening her up to criticism for shifting positions and political vulnerability on these issues.

### Votes on Major LGBTQ Legislation

#### Equality Act (2021)

**2021: Maria Elvira Salazar Voted Against The Equality Act In February** According to Miami Herald, "She said her decision to run for Congress was prompted by Salazar's vote in February against the Equality Act, a bill that would prohibit discrimination based on gender identity and sexual orientation by amending the Civil Rights Act and Fair Housing Act." \[Miami Herald, [8/4/21](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6396-RRD1-DYJM-M4TC-00000-00&context=1519360)\]

**2021: Salazar Votó Contra La Ley De Igualdad En Febrero** According to El Nuevo Herald, "Explicó que su decisión de postularse al Congreso fue impulsada por el voto de Salazar en febrero contra la Ley de Igualdad, un proyecto de ley que prohíbe la discriminación basada en la identidad de género y la orientación sexual mediante la modificación de la Ley de Derechos Civiles y la Ley de Igualdad en la Vivienda." \[El Nuevo Herald, [8/4/21](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6397-D1W1-DYJM-M02S-00000-00&context=1519360)\]

**2021: Salazar Voted Against The Equality Act** According to Miami Herald, "Perez said last month that Salazar's vote against the Equality Act, a bill that would prohibit discrimination based on gender identity and sexual orientation, was the 'straw that broke the camel's back' to prompt a run for Congress." \[Miami Herald, [9/15/21](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63M9-H951-JC3J-X2J4-00000-00&context=1519360)\]

**2021: Salazar Voted Against The Equality Act, According To Janelle Pérez** According to El Nuevo Herald, "Pérez dijo el mes pasado que el voto de Salazar en contra de la Ley de Igualdad, un proyecto de ley que prohibiría la discriminación basada en la identidad de género y la orientación sexual, fue la 'gota que derramó el vaso' para impulsar una candidatura al Congreso." \[El Nuevo Herald (Miami, Florida), [9/17/21](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63MK-FJW1-JC3J-X53Y-00000-00&context=1519360)\]

### Opposition to LGBTQ-Related Legislation

#### Reasons for Voting Against Equality Act

However, we cannot pass laws that advance one interest and bypass long-held legal protections for others.'" \[South Florida Sun-Sentinel, [12/9/22](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6723-X2D1-DY37-F1RK-00000-00&context=1519360)\]
