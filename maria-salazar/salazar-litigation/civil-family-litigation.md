# Civil and Family Litigation

### Summary

-   In 2018, María Elvira Salazar's campaign threatened legal action and sought to block the broadcast of a political attack ad using edited footage from her past Castro interview, raising questions about transparency and attempts to limit criticism ([Miami Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5SXW-8RV1-DYJM-M078-00000-00&context=1519360), [News-Journal](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5T02-MSV1-DXVP-V4WV-00000-00&context=1519360), [El Nuevo Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5SY0-4CF1-JC3J-X047-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   The attack ad controversy centered on claims that footage was deceptively edited to misrepresent Salazar's stance on the Cuban regime, but her strongly worded denials and aggressive response risked drawing more attention to the underlying criticism ([Miami Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5SXW-8RV1-DYJM-M078-00000-00&context=1519360), [El Nuevo Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5SY0-4CF1-JC3J-X047-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Salazar's campaign described the ads as defamatory and manipulated, but repeated public statements refuting the claims may have amplified the controversy and public scrutiny ([Miami Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5SY9-CC51-JC3J-X38G-00000-00&context=1519360), [News-Journal](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5T02-MSV1-DXVP-V4WV-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   In her personal life, divorce records showed a contentious property settlement and a dismissed lawsuit against Wells Fargo, which could be used to allege poor judgment or financial mismanagement ([Miami Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5TC6-DW71-JC3J-X046-00000-00&context=1519360), [El Nuevo Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5TC6-DW71-JC3J-X04B-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   A long-standing distressed mortgage---settled only in 2020 and still listed as her liability---may raise questions about her personal financial responsibility ([Miami Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63K3-15T1-JC3J-X46S-00000-00&context=1519360)).
