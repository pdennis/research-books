# Ethics in Health Policy and Campaigns

### Legal Actions and Ethics in Healthcare Sector

**November 2018: Salazar Filed IRS Complaint Over Healthcare Ad** According to Miami Herald, "Republican Congressional candidate Maria Elvira Salazar has lodged a complaint with the Internal Revenue Service accusing the left-leaning Center for Voter Information of mailing out fliers that 'lie' about her healthcare stance." \[Miami Herald, [11/2/18](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5TMT-DFH1-JC3J-X1WM-00000-00&context=1519360)\]

### Campaign Disclosures Related to Health Care

**2018: Mailer Claimed Salazar Did Not Support Pre-existing Condition Coverage** According to Miami Herald, "In a political mailer sent to a voter in District 27, the Washington, D.C.-based nonprofit states that Salazar does not 'support requiring health insurance companies to cover individuals with pre-existing conditions' and that her opponent, Democrat Donna Shalala, does." \[Miami Herald, [11/2/18](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5TMT-DFH1-JC3J-X1WM-00000-00&context=1519360)\]
