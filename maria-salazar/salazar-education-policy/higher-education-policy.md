# Higher Education Policy

### Vocational and Technical Education

#### Investment in career and technical training

**2018: Miami Herald Editorial Board Reported Maria Elvira Salazar Supported Expanding Trade Schools and Vocational Education** According to an editorial published in Miami Herald, "Salazar says that we needs more trade schools. 'We need more diesel mechanics,' she says. 'Not everyone needs to go to college.'" \[Editorial - Miami Herald, [10/14/18](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5TGN-8J71-DYJM-M15Y-00000-00&context=1519360)\]

#### Apprenticeship program support

**February 2021: Sarasota Herald Tribune Reported That Maria Elvira Salazar Voted To Expand Federally Funded Apprenticeship Programs** According to Sarasota Herald Tribune, "Voting yes: Lawson, Murphy, Soto, Demings, Crist, Castor, Hastings, Frankel, Lois, Wasserman Schultz, Wilson, Diaz-Balart, Gimenez, Salazar" regarding expanding federally funded apprenticeship programs. \[Sarasota Herald Tribune (Florida), [2/7/21](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6203-B8B1-JBCN-40NB-00000-00&context=1519360)\]
