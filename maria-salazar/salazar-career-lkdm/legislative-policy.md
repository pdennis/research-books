# Legislative Activity and Policy Positions

### Summary

-   María Elvira Salazar sponsored and supported a range of bills in Congress, including measures on tax exclusions, prohibition of non-U.S. flags at public buildings, environmental protection, and immigration reform ([BillTrack50](https://www.billtrack50.com/legislatordetail/25692#bills); [El Nuevo Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DBX-7KM1-JC3J-X3R7-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   She is known for her strong opposition to socialism and communism, authoring a House resolution denouncing socialism (H.Res. 548) and making this stance a central part of her political identity ([FreedomWorks Foundation](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63C9-B7D1-JBJN-M3X9-00000-00&context=1519360); [El Nuevo Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66PB-CSN1-JC3J-X4JH-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Salazar served as Chairwoman of the House Foreign Affairs Subcommittee on Western Hemisphere Affairs, actively advocating for democracy and human rights in Cuba, Venezuela, Nicaragua, and other Latin American countries ([Miami Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D5F-FW61-DYJM-M087-00000-00&context=1519360); [El Nuevo Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D5F-FW61-DYJM-M04X-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   She was one of the few House Republicans to vote in favor of creating a commission to investigate the January 6th Capitol attack and to raise the federal purchase age for assault-style rifles ([Miami Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DBW-K6M1-DYJM-M18D-00000-00&context=1519360); [El Nuevo Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DBX-7KM1-JC3J-X3R7-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   A significant vulnerability: Salazar repeatedly voted against major bipartisan bills, including the Bipartisan Infrastructure Law and CHIPS and Science Act, but later promoted or took credit for the funds and projects those laws brought to her district, drawing criticism ([Independent](https://www.independent.co.uk/news/world/americas/us-politics/maria-elvira-salazar-florida-republican-b2486909.html); [Miami Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D5F-FW61-DYJM-M087-00000-00&context=1519360); [Floricua News](https://floricuanews.com/2024/05/13/10-things-to-know-about-congresswoman-maria-elvira-salazar/)).
