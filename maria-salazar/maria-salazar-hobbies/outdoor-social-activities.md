# Outdoor and Social Activities

### Summary

-   In April 2023, U.S. Rep. María Elvira Salazar publicly welcomed Shakira to Miami via an enthusiastic social media post, using popular lyrics as a nod to her Latin American roots ([El Nuevo Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:67XW-NMC1-JC3J-X40G-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Salazar's post about Shakira received mixed reactions, with some constituents praising her engagement while others criticized her for focusing on celebrity welcome instead of urgent district matters ([El Nuevo Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:67XW-NMC1-JC3J-X40G-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Supporters defended Salazar's right to share personal opinions and engage in social activities outside of her official duties, emphasizing her humanity and connection with the community ([El Nuevo Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:67XW-NMC1-JC3J-X40G-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   In January 2024, Salazar participated in the Cutler Bay Chili Cook-off, joining local officials and residents in a community event and publicly thanking organizers ([Cutler Bay News (Florida)](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6B89-DPF1-DXVP-T0S9-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Potential vulnerability: Social media activity focused on celebrities may expose Salazar to criticism for not prioritizing pressing district issues, as expressed by some constituents ([El Nuevo Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:67XW-NMC1-JC3J-X40G-00000-00&context=1519360)).

### Community Engagement

#### Attending local events

**April 2023: María Elvira Salazar Publicly Welcomed Shakira To Miami On Social Media** According to El Nuevo Herald, "muchos le dan la bienvenida, entre ellos la congresista María Elvira Salazar, que le dedicó unas palabras en sus redes sociales. \[...\] 'Shakira, eres claramente bienvenida a esta ciudad y que a otro le salpique', escribió Salazar en su perfil de Instagram, destacando en mayúsculas Claramente y Salpique." \[El Nuevo Herald, [4/4/23](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:67XW-NMC1-JC3J-X40G-00000-00&context=1519360)\]

**April 2023: Salazar's Social Media Post Welcoming Shakira Received Mixed Public Reactions** According to El Nuevo Herald, "La bienvenida de Salazar a Shakira recibió críticas que la señalaban por desviarse de temas más urgentes como congresista, otros dijeron que era una forma de cortejar los votos de la comunidad colombiana y otros reconocieron el derecho de Salazar de hacer comentarios personales." \[El Nuevo Herald, [4/4/23](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:67XW-NMC1-JC3J-X40G-00000-00&context=1519360)\]

**April 2023: District 27 Constituent Criticized María Elvira Salazar's Shakira Post** According to El Nuevo Herald, "una persona que en Instagram se identifica como 'La claudeta' y dijo ser 'contribuyente del Distrito 27', que preside Salazar, mostró su desacuerdo con el comentario de la congresista. \[...\] 'Con tantos problemas en nuestra comunidad! Yo no voté por usted para estas sandeces!! De verdad!! Quién le está llevando las redes sociales a la congresista actualmente!???'" \[El Nuevo Herald, [4/4/23](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:67XW-NMC1-JC3J-X40G-00000-00&context=1519360)\]

**April 2023: María Elvira Salazar Was Defended By Supporters For Shakira Welcome Post** According to El Nuevo Herald, "Alejandra Díaz de Cubillan \[...\] defendió el derecho de las personas públicas a tener vida privada y expresar su opinión en redes. 'Ser congresista es su trabajo y creo que no está obligada a trabajar 24 horas al día y 365 días a al año, ella también tiene vida social y es un ser humano con familia como todos!! Dejen ser!!', dijo Díaz de Cubillan." \[El Nuevo Herald, [4/4/23](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:67XW-NMC1-JC3J-X40G-00000-00&context=1519360)\]

**April 2023: María Elvira Salazar Was Praised By Humberto Miyares For Supporting Shakira** According to El Nuevo Herald, "'Excelente comentario de la representante María Elvira! Shakira es bienvenida y estamos orgullosos de ser latinoamericanos!', dijo otro seguidor de la congresista, Humberto Miyares." \[El Nuevo Herald, [4/4/23](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:67XW-NMC1-JC3J-X40G-00000-00&context=1519360)\]

**January 2024: U.S. Rep. María Elvira Salazar Attended Cutler Bay Chili Cook-off** According to Cutler Bay News (Florida), "Besides residents, the event was attended by the entire Cutler Bay Council, U.S. Rep. María Elvira Salazar \[...\]" \[Cutler Bay News (Florida), [2/6/24](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6B89-DPF1-DXVP-T0S9-00000-00&context=1519360)\]

**January 2024: Rep. Salazar Thanked Organizers Of The Cutler Bay Chili Cook-off** According to Cutler Bay News (Florida), "Rep. Salazar was pleased with the event. 'I want to thank Mayor Meerbott, town manager Casals, the town council, and all the staff for throwing a great event,' Rep. Salazar said. 'A big congrats to the winners of the Cutler Bay Chili Cook-off.'" \[Cutler Bay News (Florida), [2/6/24](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6B89-DPF1-DXVP-T0S9-00000-00&context=1519360)\]
