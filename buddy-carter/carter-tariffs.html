Overview

Carter minimized Georgians’ “short term pain” and claimed that “the long term gain for American workers, for American families, will far outweigh that.” He wholly backed Trump’s tariffs and told Georgians to “hang in there and tolerate” their economic suffering. Tariffs slammed Georgians with increased housing costs and grocery bills and constricted activity at Georgia’s ports.

Carter defended Trump’s trade wars with ally nations and declared that “tariffs are the answer” to supply chain insecurities.
