# Federal Spending and Budget Policy

### Summary

> * Ashley Hinson has consistently linked rising inflation and increased costs for Iowans to federal government spending, blaming "out-of-control" or "wasteful spending" by Democrats for these challenges ([Des Moines Register](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D7B-2B91-JB1V-G17C-00000-00&context=1519360), [Waterloo-Cedar Falls Courier](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D7J-BH31-JBCN-341W-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Hinson has prioritized reducing inflation by advocating to slow government growth and spending, reduce taxes and regulatory burdens on small businesses, and invest in projects such as airport expansions that promote economic development ([Daily Nonpareil](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D7G-PMR1-JBCN-307S-00000-00&context=1519360), [Telegraph Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D96-4CJ1-JCDM-J2KG-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * She opposed increased IRS funding and broader IRS enforcement powers, voting against hiring 87,000 new IRS agents and expressing concern over audits targeting working-class Americans ([Telegraph Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:67BW-X4J1-DXVP-V455-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Hinson has supported measures to address inflation through both bipartisan and partisan approaches, including co-sponsoring anti-inflation legislation with Democrats and championing tax credits like the Child Tax Credit for working families ([Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6613-CD31-JBCN-431S-00000-00&context=1519360), [Des Moines Register](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D7B-2B91-JB1V-G17C-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * She has identified energy policy reform—especially increasing domestic energy production and streamlining permitting—as a key strategy for addressing inflation and reducing costs ([Telegraph Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DBP-38P1-JCDM-J02X-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Potential vulnerability: While Hinson calls for targeted investments and tax relief, her consistent opposition to increased IRS resources could open criticism about revenue enforcement and budget deficit impacts ([Telegraph Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:67BW-X4J1-DXVP-V455-00000-00&context=1519360)).
