# Banking, Taxation, and Financial Regulation

### Summary

> * Ashley Hinson has consistently advocated for extending and making permanent the 2017 Trump-era tax cuts, emphasizing their importance for small businesses and claiming failure to extend them would raise taxes for Iowa families ([Gazette, 4/24/25](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6FN6-XR73-S16C-00WD-00000-00), [Des Moines Register, 12/2/24](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DJG-YRY1-JB1V-G02Y-00000-00), [Sioux City Journal, 8/22/23](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:690Y-JN31-JBCN-3323-00000-00)).
> 
> * She opposed proposals to repeal 2017 corporate tax cuts, as well as efforts to increase capital gains taxes and other taxes on the wealthy, maintaining a focus on minimizing taxes for all ([Telegraph Herald, 5/26/21](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:62SD-KCW1-JCDM-J21Y-00000-00), [Gazette, 10/1/21](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63RS-X0G1-DXVP-V0GW-00000-00)).
> 
> * Hinson co-sponsored legislation to repeal the federal estate tax and has championed expanding the child tax credit for individuals and expecting parents ([Telegraph Herald, 3/14/21](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6272-P871-DYWP-80F8-00000-00), [Daily Nonpareil, 8/24/23](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6915-HS91-JBCN-321G-00000-00)).
> 
> * She has supported efforts to cut IRS funding and regulatory burdens, while opposing legislation intended to advance racial and economic equity in federal financial institutions ([Globe Gazette, 11/4/23](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:69JH-DYJ1-JBCN-31H2-00000-00), [Waterloo-Cedar Falls Courier, 8/11/22](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:664H-RB61-JBCN-43F7-00000-00)).
> 
> * Hinson has faced criticism in letters and opinion pieces for supporting tax policies perceived to primarily benefit wealthy individuals and large corporations, raising concerns about economic fairness ([Gazette, 10/23/23](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:69G4-XXK1-JBCN-30W9-00000-00), [Gazette, 4/15/22](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:657J-C681-DXVP-V4XY-00000-00)).
