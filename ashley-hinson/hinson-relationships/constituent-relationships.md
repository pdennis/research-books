# Relationships with Constituents, Local Groups, and Organizations

### Summary

> * Hinson has faced criticism for allegedly refusing meetings and not being accessible to some community members and organizations ([Courier letter](https://wcfcourier.com/opinion/letters/hinson-accomplishing-nothing-for-iowa/article_2d776c59-ce59-5b62-9559-6319939fe473.html); [Gazette editorial](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6208-YT61-DXVP-V2X5-00000-00&context=1519360); [Indivisible Iowa criticism](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63SD-VWW1-JBCN-424T-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Repeated use of divisive or partisan rhetoric and social media activity has led to accusations of name-calling and misinformation, potentially alienating constituents with differing political views ([Gazette letter](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:642P-M7T1-DXVP-V157-00000-00&context=1519360); [Gazette letter](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:663F-2F61-DXVP-V41R-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Local Democratic leaders and advocacy groups have publicly criticized Hinson for her alliances and decisions, such as inviting Sen. Ted Cruz and opposing key Iowa interests (e.g., Renewable Fuel Standard), which may undermine her support among farmers and moderates ([Des Moines Register](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63GR-GHW1-DYJJ-P231-00000-00&context=1519360); [Iowa City Press-Citizen](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63GY-FVC1-JC3H-03HV-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Some constituents and critics argue Hinson mainly meets with supporters and avoids hostile audiences, raising concerns about genuine bipartisan engagement ([Courier letter](https://wcfcourier.com/opinion/letters/hinson-accomplishing-nothing-for-iowa/article_2d776c59-ce59-5b62-9559-6319939fe473.html); [Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63SD-VWW1-JBCN-424T-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * National Democratic groups have labeled Hinson as vulnerable due to perceived alignment with far-right positions and the broader GOP agenda, which could be a liability in more competitive cycles ([Muscatine Journal](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6FHV-6X13-RSWX-N241-00000-00&context=1519360)).
