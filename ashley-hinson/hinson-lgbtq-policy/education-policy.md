# Education and School Policy

### Summary

> * Ashley Hinson consistently opposed policies that allow transgender students to use facilities matching their gender identity, including urging Linn-Mar School District to end its gender support policy and opposing changes to Title IX expanding protections for transgender students ([Radio Iowa](https://www.radioiowa.com/2022/05/05/hinson-says-linn-mar-should-end-its-gender-support-policy/), [Muscatine Journal](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DXB-SH13-RWS1-50VY-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * She strongly supported Iowa state laws and federal efforts banning transgender women and girls from participating in girls’ sports, repeatedly praising the actions of Gov. Kim Reynolds and Republican lawmakers ([Des Moines Register](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DC5-P5C1-DXGX-61F4-00000-00&context=1519360), [Telegraph Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D96-4CJ1-JCDM-J2KG-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Hinson justified her position on transgender participation in girls’ sports by citing fairness, the "biological differences" between men and women, and protecting the integrity and progress of women’s athletics ([Daily Nonpareil](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DXJ-RJD3-RRYM-93FK-00000-00&context=1519360), [Globe Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DXB-SH13-RWJS-W4TJ-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * She opposed the Biden administration’s efforts to expand Title IX protections for transgender students and praised legal rulings that blocked such changes ([Globe Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DXB-SH13-RWJS-W4TJ-00000-00&context=1519360), [Daily Nonpareil](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DXJ-RJD3-RRYM-93FK-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Hinson raised concerns about parental notification policies, arguing that schools should inform parents if students seek to change their gender identity or pronouns, framing it as a matter of parental rights and student well-being ([Radio Iowa](https://www.radioiowa.com/2022/05/05/hinson-says-linn-mar-should-end-its-gender-support-policy/)).
