# Income and Assets

### Summary

> * Ashley Hinson reported earning $32,801.73 in 2020 from her salary and per diem as a member of the Iowa House of Representatives, where she served until 2021 ([Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63MT-8G31-DXVP-V2FM-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * She and her husband received significant dividends—between $100,001 and $1 million—in 2020 from Transportation Insurance & Consultants Inc. ([Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63MT-8G31-DXVP-V2FM-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Hinson disclosed interest or dividends from investments in several companies, including Apple, Constellation Brands, Gray Television, Heron Therapeutics, and QCR Holdings ([Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63MT-8G31-DXVP-V2FM-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Starting in 2021, her annual salary as a U.S. House Representative increased to $174,000, plus benefits ([Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:616Y-WJC1-JBCN-446W-00000-00&context=1519360); [Sioux City Journal](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6171-J0D1-JBCN-444S-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * She reported $5,001–$15,000 in annual rental income from property housing Elliott Hartman Insurance Services, her husband's Waterloo firm, and listed liabilities including a $50,001–$100,000 mortgage and $15,001–$50,000 business liability ([Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63MT-8G31-DXVP-V2FM-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * As of October 2021, Hinson held $1.43 million in campaign cash on hand ([Des Moines Register](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63WM-1DF1-DYJJ-P4JX-00000-00&context=1519360)).
