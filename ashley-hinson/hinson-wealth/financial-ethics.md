# Financial Ethics and Transparency

### Summary

> * In August 2021, Ashley Hinson filed her first federal financial disclosure statement as a freshman U.S. Representative, in accordance with congressional requirements ([Gazette, 9/17/21](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63MT-8G31-DXVP-V2FM-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Following media scrutiny of congressional stock trades, Hinson and her husband announced and completed the sale of all individual stocks in April 2021, retaining investments only in widely held mutual funds and ETFs for retirement ([Telegraph Herald, 4/25/21](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:62J7-F0S1-JCDM-J3W7-00000-00&context=1519360); [Gazette, 9/17/21](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63MT-8G31-DXVP-V2FM-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Hinson stated that her decision to divest individual stocks was voluntary and aligned with her campaign promises to restore public trust and ensure her work in Congress was free from conflicts of interest ([Telegraph Herald, 4/25/21](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:62J7-F0S1-JCDM-J3W7-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * In 2019, Democratic critics claimed Hinson had "thousands of dollars" invested in pharmaceutical companies, but her campaign clarified the amount was about $1,000 in a company developing opioid alternatives ([Gazette, 12/12/19](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5XR8-78P1-DXVP-V0V0-00000-00&context=1519360); [Waterloo-Cedar Falls Courier, 12/13/19](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5XRP-5N51-DXVP-V0M4-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Potential vulnerability: Hinson’s past investments in drug companies, though relatively small and since divested, were used by opponents to question her financial transparency and ethical standards.

### Stock Holdings and Trading Practices

#### <span style="text-decoration: underline; border-bottom: 1px solid #999; padding-bottom: 2px;">Divestment from Individual Stocks</span>

**April 2021: Ashley Hinson Announced Plans To Sell Individual Stocks Not Part Of Retirement Funds After Media Reports On Congressional Stock Trades** According to Gazette, "Hinson told the Telegraph Herald in April she and her husband would sell stocks that weren't part of widely used mutual funds and exchange-traded funds for their retirement. This was after it was reported some U.S. senators and representatives made money on pandemic-related stock trades." [Gazette (Cedar Rapids, IA), [9/17/21](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63MT-8G31-DXVP-V2FM-00000-00&context=1519360)]

**2021: Ashley Hinson's Office Claimed She And Husband Sold All Individual Stocks And Would Only Invest In Mutual Funds Or ETFs** According to Gazette, "Hinson spokeswoman Sophie Seid said this week Hinson and Arenholz have since sold all their individual stocks. 'While she is in Congress, they will not actively buy or sell any individual publicly traded stocks, and they will only invest in widely held mutual funds and ETFs (Exchange Traded Fund) for retirement savings,' Seid said in an email. 'This isn't a requirement for members of Congress, they did so voluntarily.'" [Gazette (Cedar Rapids, IA), [9/17/21](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63MT-8G31-DXVP-V2FM-00000-00&context=1519360)]

**April 2021: Ashley Hinson Announced Stock Divestment To Ensure Public Trust** According to Telegraph Herald, "U.S. Rep. Ashley Hinson, R-Iowa, announced Friday that both she and her husband, Matt Hinson, would sell all their publicly traded stocks to better ensure public trust." **Ashley Hinson Cited Campaign Promises As Motivation For Stock Sale In 2021** According to Telegraph Herald, "Hinson said Friday that her family selling their shares aligned with campaign promises to help 'restore integrity and trust in public service.'" [Telegraph Herald (Dubuque, IA), [4/25/21](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:62J7-F0S1-JCDM-J3W7-00000-00&context=1519360)]

**Ashley Hinson Asserted Her Actions Were To Serve Iowa Constituents Without Doubt In 2021** According to Telegraph Herald, "My constituents deserve to have full confidence that my efforts in Washington are guided only by what is best for our district," Hinson said. "There should never be any doubt that I'm here to serve only the people of Iowa."
