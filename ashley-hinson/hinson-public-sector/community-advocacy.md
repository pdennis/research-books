# Community Engagement and Public Policy Advocacy

### Summary

> * Ashley Hinson has prioritized accessibility and constituent engagement throughout her public service, holding over 40 in-person and virtual town halls across her congressional district, often emphasizing her commitment to hearing from all Iowans regardless of political affiliation ([Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6F9R-RXV3-RRW3-X06S-00000-00&context=1519360), [Telegraph Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:64BV-P6V1-DXVP-V187-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> 
> * She regularly forms and consults constituent advisory groups—including coalitions for farmers, veterans, law enforcement, and small businesses—to guide her policy decisions and ensure feedback informs her legislative priorities ([Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:61BC-DF71-DXVP-V07D-00000-00&context=1519360), [Waterloo-Cedar Falls Courier](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:61BD-MRS1-DXVP-V2WD-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> 
> * Hinson actively pursues federal funding and support for numerous local projects, including infrastructure, flood protection, education, health care, and economic development; she has secured or requested millions in earmarks for Iowa communities ([Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6C77-27C1-JBCN-3122-00000-00&context=1519360), [Bulletin-Review](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CD9-JT01-DXVP-T3Y4-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> 
> * Consistent with her background in journalism and broadcasting, Hinson emphasizes the importance of transparency and regular communication, holding weekly press calls and inviting unfiltered public questions at town halls ([Telegraph Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:619P-9VV1-JCDM-J4M3-00000-00&context=1519360), [Globe Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6FMT-TD63-RSVX-V43P-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> 
> * She has received both praise and criticism for her approach—supporters highlight her accessibility and bipartisanship, while critics sometimes challenge her willingness to directly debate difficult issues and note selective attendance at debates or meetings ([Globe Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6C59-9HN1-JBCN-341H-00000-00&context=1519360), [Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5VSW-GX41-JBCN-44GH-00000-00&context=1519360), [Bleeding Heartland](https://www.bleedingheartland.com/2020/07/01/ashley-hinson-dodged-iowa-house-debates-on-high-profile-bills/)).
> 
> * Vulnerability: Hinson has faced criticism from some constituents and local media who allege she avoids or deflects tough questions at public forums, and there are letters and reports questioning her bipartisanship and overall accessibility to all constituent groups ([Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5VSW-GX41-JBCN-44GH-00000-00&context=1519360), [Waterloo-Cedar Falls Courier](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63FG-2D71-JBCN-43J4-00000-00&context=1519360)).
