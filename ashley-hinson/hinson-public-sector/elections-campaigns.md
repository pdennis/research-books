# Elections and Political Campaigns

### Summary

> * Ashley Hinson faced plagiarism allegations during her 2020 congressional campaign, resulting in her public apology, termination of a consulting firm, and questions about her campaign's integrity and oversight ([KCCI](https://www.kcci.com/article/republican-democrat-iowa-politics-election-ashley-hinson-apologizes-for-plagiarism/33409863), [Des Moines Register](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:60DT-8XK1-JC3H-04F6-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Critics and political opponents have characterized Hinson’s voting record as highly partisan, frequently aligning with party interests over bipartisan solutions or constituent priorities ([Daily Nonpareil](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D7G-PMR1-JBCN-307T-00000-00&context=1519360), [Telegraph Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D96-4CJ1-JCDM-J2KJ-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Hinson has faced ongoing criticism from activists and letter writers for opposing major Democratic legislation like the American Rescue Plan Act, as well as for employing polarizing rhetoric against Democrats in campaign fundraising and public messages ([Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:62BP-WBM1-JBCN-41BH-00000-00&context=1519360), [Iowa City Press-Citizen](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63BP-27F1-DYJJ-P0P6-00000-00&context=1519360), [Waterloo-Cedar Falls Courier](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:62P4-XB41-DXVP-V4C8-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Her relatively narrow win in 2020 and the swing nature of her district have repeatedly made her a target for Democratic challengers and national committees, who continue to see her seat as competitive despite her growing margins in subsequent elections ([Iowa City Press-Citizen](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6FP2-G8V3-RTDH-T4SN-00000-00&context=1519360), [Sioux City Journal](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6FJ2-6303-RTJS-40C3-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Some supporters and commentators have questioned whether Hinson’s growing profile and national ambitions could reduce her focus on district issues or alienate moderate voters, especially as she aligns closely with party leadership and former President Trump’s agenda ([Des Moines Register](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DD7-J371-DXGX-6505-00000-00&context=1519360), [Waterloo-Cedar Falls Courier](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DDM-H1G1-DXVP-T0GJ-00000-00&context=1519360)).
