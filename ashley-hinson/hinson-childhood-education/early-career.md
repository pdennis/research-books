# Early Career Foundations

### Summary

> * Ashley Hinson began her professional career as a news anchor at KCRG-TV, establishing a background in broadcast journalism ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ashley_Hinson)).
> * Before her role in Congress, Hinson worked as a TV news journalist and served as a state representative in the Iowa House ([Des Moines Register](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DB9-TH21-DXGX-622M-00000-00&context=1519360); [Mitchell County Press-News](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:602C-V341-DXVP-V26W-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * She returned to Iowa in 2005 to raise her family and advance her career in both journalism and public service ([NBC - 13 WHO](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:617D-5J81-DXVP-V3PW-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Hinson cited her commitment to community engagement and effective communication as key motivations for entering politics ([Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5HK8-WR81-F0NV-F3BH-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * By 2020, she was recognized as a two-term state representative from Marion with significant experience in both broadcast journalism and state government ([Iowa City Press-Citizen](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6176-BK41-DYJJ-P125-00000-00&context=1519360)).
