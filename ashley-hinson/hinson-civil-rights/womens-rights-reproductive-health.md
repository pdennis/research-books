# Women's Rights and Reproductive Health

### Summary

> * Multiple letters to the editor in 2021 and 2022 claimed that Ashley Hinson voted against the Violence Against Women Act, the Equal Rights Amendment, and equal pay for women ([Courier](https://wcfcourier.com/opinion/letters/hinson-accomplishing-nothing-for-iowa/article_2d776c59-ce59-5b62-9559-6319939fe473.html), [Waterloo-Cedar Falls Courier](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63FG-2D71-JBCN-43J6-00000-00&context=1519360), [Gazette (Cedar Rapids)](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66K9-5D31-DXVP-V07F-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Hinson has stated support for a "clean" Republican version of the Violence Against Women Act reauthorization, opposing what she described as "partisan additions" included in Democratic versions ([Waterloo-Cedar Falls Courier](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66SF-8R71-DXVP-V30S-00000-00&context=1519360), [Gazette (Cedar Rapids)](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66SD-FSJ1-DXVP-V548-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * She has publicly advocated for fairness in women's athletics, arguing against allowing transgender girls to compete with cisgender girls based on her beliefs about biological differences ([Waterloo-Cedar Falls Courier](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DXF-FRR3-S016-V13N-00000-00&context=1519360), [Sioux City Journal](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DXJ-RJD3-RRYM-94HP-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Public accusations included her opposition to extending protections for women and mothers, such as the Paycheck Fairness Act, raising the minimum wage, access to contraception, and extending the child tax credit ([Gazette (Cedar Rapids)](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66K9-5D31-DXVP-V07F-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Hinson was rated by OnTheIssues.org as opposing measures to legally require hiring of women and minorities, and voted to keep deadlines for the Equal Rights Amendment ratification ([OnTheIssues.org](https://ontheissues.org/House/Ashley_Hinson_Civil_Rights.htm#21-HJR17)).
