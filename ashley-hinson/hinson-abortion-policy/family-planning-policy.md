# Family Planning and Birth Control Policy

### Summary

> * Hinson voted against federal legislation to guarantee a nationwide right to access contraception, drawing criticism from opponents who say her alternative bills are weaker and don't provide comprehensive protections ([Gazette, 10/26/24](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D8M-KY71-DXVP-T2V4-00000-00&context=1519360), [Sioux City Journal, 10/26/24](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D8S-HS31-JBCN-334J-00000-00), [Muscatine Journal, 10/29/24](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D98-Y7P1-JBCN-31DJ-00000-00))
> * Her bills for over-the-counter (OTC) birth control access limit eligibility to women 18 and older, omitting teens—an age group often at risk—raising concerns about youth birth control access ([The Gazette, 6/23/24](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CB5-TSG1-DXVP-T05B-00000-00&context=1519360), [Daily Nonpareil, 6/18/24](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6C8X-TPM1-JBCN-30YW-00000-00&context=1519360))
> * Critics accuse Hinson of political inconsistency, highlighting her past support for Planned Parenthood’s routine care and subsequent shift in stance, as well as claims of supporting IVF while co-sponsoring anti-IVF legislation ([Gazette, 8/6/20](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:60J1-J221-DXVP-V2D9-00000-00&context=1519360), [Iowa City Press-Citizen, 5/21/24](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6C2X-GHT1-JB1V-G0HD-00000-00&context=1519360))
> * Hinson’s proposed policies on contraception focus primarily on oral contraceptives and lack guaranteed protections against state-level restrictions, leaving gaps in access for some women and limiting birth control method choices ([The Gazette, 6/23/24](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CB5-TSG1-DXVP-T05B-00000-00&context=1519360))
> * Hinson consistently faces attacks from Democratic opponents for allegedly voting against women's reproductive rights and for not supporting broad, federal protections for contraception and IVF access ([Gazette, 10/26/24](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D8M-KY71-DXVP-T2V4-00000-00&context=1519360), [Iowa City Press-Citizen, 5/21/24](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6C2X-GHT1-JB1V-G0HD-00000-00&context=1519360))
