# Maternal and Child Health Initiatives

### Summary

> * Ashley Hinson sponsored and led the bipartisan Maternal and Child Health Stillbirth Prevention Act, which allows federal resources to be used for stillbirth prevention, education, and research; the bill passed Congress in 2024, aiming to address the 20,000+ annual U.S. stillbirths ([Globe Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6C29-R3Y1-JBCN-30G0-00000-00&context=1519360), [Daily Nonpareil](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6C29-R3X1-JBCN-3526-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Hinson co-introduced the Providing for Life Act, which proposes expanding the child tax credit, extending WIC eligibility for postpartum and breastfeeding mothers, enabling early access to Social Security benefits for parental leave, and allowing federal funds for faith-based and community pregnancy centers ([Telegraph Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66FD-H8B1-DYWP-817V-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * She spearheaded the Pregnant Students Rights Act, requiring colleges to inform pregnant students of protections, available resources, flexible schedules, childcare assistance, and worked to address mental health challenges associated with pregnancy on campus ([Globe Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6B5K-86V1-JBCN-34NC-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Hinson has prioritized initiatives to reduce maternal health care deserts, expand access to midwives and birthing centers, and improve care in rural areas, including securing federal funding for maternity unit expansions at Iowa hospitals ([Globe Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6C29-R3Y1-JBCN-30G0-00000-00&context=1519360), [Daily Nonpareil](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D6R-9281-DXVP-T2GS-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Hinson has consistently linked her pro-life stance to supporting families beyond birth—emphasizing child care access, parental leave, accountability for fathers’ pregnancy costs, and making child/adoption tax credits more accessible ([Sioux City Journal](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:68SY-2T41-JBCN-33BP-00000-00&context=1519360), [Muscatine Journal](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:68SY-2T41-JBCN-34K1-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Potential vulnerability: While supportive of expanding the child tax credit, Hinson has voted against its expansion when included in Democrat-backed legislation, such as the American Rescue Plan Act ([Telegraph Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66FD-H8B1-DYWP-817V-00000-00&context=1519360)).
