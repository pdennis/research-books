# Legislative Actions and Voting Record on Guns

### Summary

> * Ashley Hinson has consistently opposed major federal gun control measures, voting against the Bipartisan Safer Communities Act, Bipartisan Background Checks Act of 2021 (H.R. 8), the Bipartisan Protecting Our Kids Act 2022, and the Bipartisan Active Shooter Alert Act 2022 ([Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D6G-X7Y1-JBCN-349J-00000-00&context=1519360), [Telegraph Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6B4M-JWB1-DYWP-8082-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * She has co-sponsored and supported legislation promoting concealed carry reciprocity between states and sought to prevent federal gun control via public health emergencies ([Daily Nonpareil](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CT0-WVG1-DXVP-T3XS-00000-00&context=1519360), [Waterloo-Cedar Falls Courier](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CSS-H791-DXVP-T2VD-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Hinson has also co-sponsored bills to remove short-barreled rifles from the National Firearms Act and reverse ATF rules on stabilizing braces, arguing these changes support home defense and disabled veterans ([Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CSS-H791-DXVP-T06M-00000-00&context=1519360), [Globe Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CSJ-YJ21-JBCN-30G7-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * She has publicly opposed what she calls “sweeping anti-firearm regulations” from the Biden-Harris administration, frequently emphasizing protection of Second Amendment rights and concerns about due process in gun legislation ([Globe Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CSJ-YJ21-JBCN-30G7-00000-00&context=1519360), [Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:65S8-9KJ1-DXVP-V229-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Hinson’s resistance to bipartisan gun safety legislation and enhanced background checks presents potential vulnerabilities with constituents favoring stronger gun violence prevention measures ([Manchester Press](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6B48-YSV1-DXVP-T1CX-00000-00&context=1519360), [Telegraph Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6B4M-JWB1-DYWP-80J2-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * In moments addressing mass shootings, Hinson has focused on bolstering law enforcement and mental health support rather than endorsing new gun control laws, reiterating her stance on balancing Second Amendment rights with public safety ([Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6B1P-YY21-JBCN-33P0-00000-00&context=1519360), [Manchester Press](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:680J-YRK1-DXVP-T54G-00000-00&context=1519360)).
