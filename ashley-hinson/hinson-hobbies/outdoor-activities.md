# Outdoor and Recreational Activities

### Summary

> * Ashley Hinson resumed horseback riding during the COVID-19 pandemic, describing it as her "sanctuary" and a source of mental reprieve ([WBALTV](https://www.wbaltv.com/article/ashley-hinson-horseback-riding/36438090), 5/15/21).
> * She not only rode horses but also volunteered at Gaitaway Stables to care for them during this period ([WBALTV](https://www.wbaltv.com/article/ashley-hinson-horseback-riding/36438090), 5/15/21).
> * Hinson emphasized the physical and mental health benefits of horseback riding, pointing out that it provides both a workout and a therapeutic outlet ([WBALTV](https://www.wbaltv.com/article/ashley-hinson-horseback-riding/36438090), 5/15/21).
