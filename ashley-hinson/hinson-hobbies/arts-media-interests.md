# Arts, Media, and Personal Interests

### Summary

> * Ashley Hinson is a classically trained violinist and pianist who has played for her church's worship team and with the Des Moines Symphony ([Fox News](https://www.foxnews.com/politics/ashley-hinson-former-tv-news-anchor-focus-taxpayers-congress), [Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5HK8-WR81-F0NV-F3BH-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * She frequently performs at campaign and community events, often leading the national anthem and playing patriotic and country tunes on her violin, notably at annual BBQ Bash fundraisers and local gatherings ([Des Moines Register](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63GR-GHW1-DYJJ-P231-00000-00&context=1519360), [Iowa City Press-Citizen](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CNB-7JX1-JB1V-G39P-00000-00&context=1519360), [Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:68WH-8KK1-JBCN-31D4-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Hinson has used her musical talents for outreach, such as producing an ad featuring her violin performance of "Amazing Grace" and a message of unity following a severe Iowa storm ([Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:60ND-6N21-JBCN-4149-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * She has advocated for music therapy, supporting certification reforms, and credits both her own and her children’s involvement in music for highlighting the therapeutic benefits ([Telegraph Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:67JS-CXX1-DYWP-817P-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Hinson remains actively engaged with local community organizations as a volunteer and performer, reinforcing her public persona as both a politician and musician ([Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5HK8-WR81-F0NV-F3BH-00000-00&context=1519360)).
