### Summary

> * Hinson's repeated self-description as a "recovering journalist" was publicly criticized and seen as dismissive of her former profession, offending some journalists and opening her up to charges of opportunism ([Gazette July 2019](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5WMR-DHD1-DXVP-V3XY-00000-00&context=1519360), [Waterloo-Cedar Falls Courier March 2020](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5YBN-CGM1-DXVP-V0JR-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Critics and letter writers have accused Hinson of hypocrisy for both leveraging and disparaging her journalism background, raising questions about her consistency and trustworthiness ([Gazette July 2019](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:5WMR-DHD1-DXVP-V3XY-00000-00&context=1519360)).
