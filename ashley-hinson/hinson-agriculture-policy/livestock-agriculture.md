# Livestock and Animal Agriculture

### Summary

> * Ashley Hinson has repeatedly opposed California agricultural regulations like Proposition 12, arguing they unfairly impact Iowa livestock producers and violate interstate commerce, introducing and co-sponsoring the EATS Act in response ([Manchester Press](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:692D-WDN1-DXVP-T250-00000-00&context=1519360); [Telegraph Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6918-P7K1-JCDM-J0GX-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Hinson emphasizes protecting producers' autonomy in animal-raising practices, stating her legislative efforts would not restrict how producers raise livestock but aim to prevent out-of-state mandates ([Telegraph Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6915-2CW1-DXVP-T280-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * She has consistently criticized federal and California-driven overregulation in agriculture, advocating for fewer EPA rules on farmers and more producer flexibility ([Des Moines Register](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66JN-RPG1-JB1V-G0F3-00000-00&context=1519360); [Globe Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66K3-HY61-DXVP-V06W-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Hinson has prioritized federal funding for animal health research and solutions to livestock disease outbreaks, notably advocating for increased bird flu research funding and highlighting USDA’s $1 billion avian influenza response plan ([Des Moines Register](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DMV-MX21-DXGX-62YN-00000-00&context=1519360); [Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6FGR-HXX3-S8TT-W067-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> * Some critics argue that Hinson’s support for the EATS Act favors large corporate agriculture interests at the expense of existing environmental, health, and safety standards ([Letter to the Editor - Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6B78-6RX1-JBCN-30DW-00000-00&context=1519360)).
