# Agricultural Policy and Rural Development

### Summary

> * Ashley Hinson has consistently advocated for Iowa farmers and rural communities, prioritizing issues like reducing input costs, protecting crop insurance, expanding access to precision agriculture, and investing in rural infrastructure and disaster relief.  
>   [Des Moines Register](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D7B-2B91-JB1V-G17C-00000-00&context=1519360), [Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66F7-PX31-DXVP-V47J-00000-00&context=1519360)
> 
> * Hinson strongly opposes policies like California's Proposition 12, which she argues threaten interstate commerce and Midwest agricultural markets, and has repeatedly introduced or co-sponsored the EATS Act to overturn such regulations.  
>   [Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:688N-H4W1-JBCN-30S5-00000-00&context=1519360), [Telegraph Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6897-F2Y1-JCDM-J0R4-00000-00&context=1519360)
> 
> * A major focus has been increasing oversight and restrictions on foreign ownership of U.S. farmland, especially by Chinese entities, framing it as a national security issue and advocating multiple bills to enforce stricter rules.  
>   [Daily Nonpareil](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6B8J-VV91-JBCN-31BY-00000-00&context=1519360), [Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:679R-KF71-JBCN-430V-00000-00&context=1519360)
> 
> * Hinson has faced some criticism or skepticism from local stakeholders regarding her positions on mandatory country-of-origin labeling (COOL) and accusations of misrepresenting aspects of federal family farm tax policy, suggesting potential vulnerabilities in her record.  
>   [Gazette Letter to the Editor](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6B78-6RX1-JBCN-30DW-00000-00&context=1519360)
> 
> * She has played a leadership role in House efforts to pass a new Farm Bill—emphasizing bipartisan solutions and criticizing congressional gridlock—and has supported temporary extensions and funding measures to maintain stability for farmers amid legislative delays.  
>   [Waterloo-Cedar Falls Courier](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D3G-X591-DXVP-T3FV-00000-00&context=1519360), [Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D3G-X591-DXVP-T153-00000-00&context=1519360)
> 
> * Hinson has been a vocal supporter of Iowa’s biofuels and renewable energy industries, rural economic development, and investments in food security and safety, often working with local stakeholders and farmer organizations.  
>   [Charles City Press](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CT0-2SF1-DXVP-T1C2-00000-00&context=1519360), [Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CYF-FS81-DXVP-T0GK-00000-00&context=1519360)
