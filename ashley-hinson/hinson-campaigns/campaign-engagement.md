# Campaign Interactions, Events, and Community Engagement

### Summary

> * Ashley Hinson has prioritized grassroots engagement through frequent town halls, direct meetings with constituents, and large-scale events such as her annual “BBQ Bash” fundraiser, which consistently attracts prominent Republican speakers and hundreds of attendees ([Gazette](https://www.thegazette.com/campaigns-elections/campaign-almanac-ashleys-army-returns-for-2022-race/), [Daily Nonpareil](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CNG-X481-JBCN-315V-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> 
> * Her campaign emphasizes a strong volunteer network known as “Ashley’s Army,” with Hinson crediting this operation and grassroots supporters for her electoral successes ([Gazette](https://www.thegazette.com/campaigns-elections/campaign-almanac-ashleys-army-returns-for-2022-race/), [Hinson Campaign Press Release](https://ashleyhinson.com/ashley-hinson-launches-ashleys-army-announces-more-than-450-grassroots-leaders-backing-her-campaign/)).
> 
> * Hinson has consistently leveraged campaign events to spotlight national and local issues, involve high-profile Republican figures, and host candidate forums, notably inviting GOP presidential hopefuls to address Iowans during election cycles ([Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:68WH-8KK1-JBCN-31D4-00000-00&context=1519360), [Daily Nonpareil](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:68WJ-1NT1-JBCN-30VT-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> 
> * Debates and public forums have played a key role, with Hinson participating in televised debates during her campaigns when possible, though some scheduled debates were canceled due to health issues or mutual scheduling conflicts ([Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66N0-XXP1-JBCN-41YN-00000-00&context=1519360), [Telegraph Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D09-BK11-JBCN-33TM-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> 
> * Hinson has faced vulnerabilities, including a 2020 plagiarism controversy involving campaign materials, for which she apologized and pledged staff accountability, and periodic criticism for declining certain high-profile public speaking invitations or for perceived politicization of disaster response efforts ([Quad-City Times](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:60DD-1TG1-DXVP-V4W3-00000-00&context=1519360), [Globe Gazette](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CRV-JYN1-JBCN-33F7-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> 
> * Community engagement also includes hands-on volunteering, participation in local events, and direct outreach to businesses and residents, reinforcing Hinson's emphasis on personal connection and responsiveness ([Telegraph Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DBP-38P1-JCDM-J02X-00000-00&context=1519360), [Southeast Iowa Union](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6FKH-5FJ3-RSVP-V1MJ-00000-00&context=1519360)).
