# Oversight, Bipartisanship, and Legislative Accountability

### Summary

> * Ashley Hinson consistently advocates for rigorous oversight of federal programs, emphasizing the need to distinguish between necessary and wasteful government spending and supporting the Department of Government Efficiency's (DOGE) efforts to root out fraud, waste, and abuse ([Telegraph Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6F25-SBM3-S6DD-9447-00000-00&context=1519360), [Sioux City Journal](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6F9C-SM13-RRW2-52MB-00000-00&context=1519360), [Des Moines Register](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6F96-04H3-S11R-S1GG-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> 
> * Hinson expresses strong support for bipartisanship in tackling national issues such as the federal debt and budgeting, noting that both parties have contributed to these challenges and must work together to resolve them ([Manchester Press](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6B2S-62Y1-DXVP-T4XV-00000-00&context=1519360), [Telegraph Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6335-D8D1-DYWP-805S-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> 
> * She has prioritized timely legislative action, criticizing Congress for delays in passing appropriations and stressing the importance of fulfilling legislative responsibilities before a new session begins ([Manchester Press](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CWJ-2VT1-JBCN-32ST-00000-00&context=1519360), [Daily Nonpareil](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6BN4-SHN1-JBCN-3104-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> 
> * Hinson supports legislative accountability measures, including requesting her congressional pay be withheld during potential government shutdowns to share the impact of such political stalemates with constituents ([Daily Nonpareil](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6992-FK01-JBCN-33S4-00000-00&context=1519360), [Sioux City Journal](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6991-4WJ1-DXVP-T1VV-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> 
> * She upholds transparency and public scrutiny regarding both executive and legislative budgets, calling for equal review of presidential and conservative proposals and openly communicating her positions and votes ([Waterloo-Cedar Falls Courier](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6BMB-HRC1-DXVP-T2CT-00000-00&context=1519360), [Sioux City Journal](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6BMB-20Y1-JBCN-3141-00000-00&context=1519360)).
