# Constituent Services and Local Engagement

### Summary

> * Ashley Hinson has actively engaged with local disability care providers and community leaders, visiting organizations like Imagine the Possibilities in Maquoketa and Industrial Rehab in Waverly to discuss workforce challenges and accessibility efforts ([Telegraph Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:637D-TP01-JCDM-J41G-00000-00&context=1519360), [Waterloo Courier](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CP1-4G51-DY8S-B474-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> 
> * In 2023, Hinson partnered with TSA to organize service dog screening demonstrations and address travel barriers faced by veterans and people with disabilities using service animals ([Daily Nonpareil](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:692N-B3V1-DXVP-T1FH-00000-00&context=1519360), [Waterloo-Cedar Falls Courier](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:692M-H1V1-JBCN-3318-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> 
> * Hinson highlighted the importance of rural access to care facilities, emphasizing that people in small towns should have equitable access to rehabilitation services and community amenities ([Waterloo Courier](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CP1-4G51-DY8S-B474-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> 
> * She showed support for local accessibility projects, such as the disability-friendly Jesup splash pad, pledging to help secure grants for its expansion and recognizing its value for community development ([Waterloo Courier](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CP1-4G51-DY8S-B474-00000-00&context=1519360), [Waterloo-Cedar Falls Courier](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CP9-YMJ1-JBCN-310T-00000-00&context=1519360)).
> 
> * Potential vulnerability: Multiple letters to the editor in 2021 criticized Hinson for not responding to constituent meeting requests related to disability issues, raising concerns about accessibility and transparency in her constituent services ([Waterloo-Cedar Falls Courier](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63F2-27P1-DXVP-V0T7-00000-00&context=1519360), [Des Moines Register](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:63F1-R6B1-JC3H-02X0-00000-00&context=1519360)).
