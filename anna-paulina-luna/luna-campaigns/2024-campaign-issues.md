# 2024 Campaign Focus and Issues

### Summary

-   National and local Democrats targeted Anna Paulina Luna's seat as a top pickup opportunity due to her MAGA-aligned approach in a competitive district ([Tampa Bay Times](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6C23-3BM1-DYTM-N003-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Luna faced criticism from opponents and even some GOP voters for partisanship and contributing to government gridlock, opening her to "extremist" or "divisive" labels ([Tampa Bay Times](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:69G5-3GH1-DYTM-N003-00000-00&context=1519360), [Washington Post](https://www.washingtonpost.com/politics/2024/11/01/anna-paulina-luna-whitney-fox/)).

-   Multiple Democratic contenders ran against Luna, fueling a strong and sustained challenge and adding to her "potentially vulnerable" status ([Tampa Bay Times](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6C23-3BM1-DYTM-N003-00000-00&context=1519360), [NBC - 8 WFLA](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D0P-MVT1-JBCN-32J5-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Luna was repeatedly criticized by Democratic opponents for voting against local district interests and for her handling of FEMA funds and other key issues ([Washington Post](https://www.washingtonpost.com/politics/2024/11/01/anna-paulina-luna-whitney-fox/), [Tampa Bay Times](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6BY3-GS31-JCBJ-Y4F4-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Some Republican voters expressed personal frustration with Luna, acknowledging her divisiveness but reluctantly continuing to support her, which could signal future weakening of her base ([Washington Post](https://www.washingtonpost.com/politics/2024/11/01/anna-paulina-luna-whitney-fox/)).
