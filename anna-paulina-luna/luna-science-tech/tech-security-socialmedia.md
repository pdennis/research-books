# Technology, Digital Security, and Social Media

### Summary

-   Anna Paulina Luna has strongly advocated for a national ban on TikTok, citing its use by the Chinese Communist Party for intelligence gathering, psychological operations, and as a national security threat ([Fox News](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:67VH-VXH1-JBHT-D315-00000-00&context=1519360), [Fox News Channel](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:67VF-1VG1-JDPT-T126-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   She introduced and supported legislation to prohibit foreign adversaries from accessing or storing American user data via social media platforms, and specifically targeted TikTok, Facebook, and Instagram ([Mornings with Maria](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:69MW-7D41-F072-X4D2-00000-00&context=1519360), [FBN](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:69N3-6MP1-DY0W-40BS-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Luna has publicly stated that she does not use social media apps on her personal phone due to digital security concerns ([Fox News](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:67VB-4XR1-F072-X3WN-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   She has aggressively pushed for federal transparency around unidentified aerial phenomena (UAPs), criticizing the Pentagon and federal agencies for withholding information from Congress and supporting legislation to automatically declassify related records ([NBC - 8 WFLA](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:69S6-2BT1-DXVP-T02D-00000-00&context=1519360), [Tampa Bay Times](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:69S6-63N1-JCBJ-Y0KV-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   In 2023-2024, Luna led a successful House amendment to ban taxpayer funding for research conducted by the People's Republic of China, emphasizing her focus on blocking technological and informational vulnerabilities ([Tampa Bay Times](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6DBR-K961-F03F-K42D-00000-00&context=1519360)).
