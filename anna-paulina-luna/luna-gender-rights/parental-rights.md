### Push for parent proxy voting in Congress

**2024: Luna Asserted That Being A New Mother Should Not Disqualify Congressional Representation** According to Florida Politics, "Being a new mom does not make you ineligible to represent the people who duly elected you," Luna said. "It's time that Congress gets with the times so that new mothers, chosen by their voters to represent them, can do their jobs." \[Florida Politics, [1/17/24](https://floridapolitics.com/archives/653497-anna-paulina-luna-wants-new-mothers-in-congress-able-to-vote-by-proxy/)\]
