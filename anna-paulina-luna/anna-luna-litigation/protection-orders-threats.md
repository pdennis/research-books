# Personal Protection Orders and Threat Allegations

### Summary

-   Anna Paulina Luna has repeatedly filed restraining orders and legal complaints against political opponents, including sensational allegations of murder plots, which some critics claim may be a political tactic or "scam" ([American Action News](https://americanactionnews.com/media/2021/10/18/anna-paulina-lunas-150000-dirty-trick-rs-fg/), [WMNF](https://www.wmnf.org/florida-gop-congressional-candidate-says-republican-opponent-conspired-to-kill-her/)).

-   Some complaints have relied on questionable evidence, such as illegally recorded calls, undermining legal proceedings and raising doubts about the credibility of her allegations ([American Action News](https://americanactionnews.com/media/2021/10/18/anna-paulina-lunas-150000-dirty-trick-rs-fg/)).

-   Luna has publicized her experiences with threats and doxxing, emphasizing her vulnerability as a female member of Congress, but high-profile reporting of these issues might prompt concerns about her security and campaign stability ([CQ Transcriptions](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D0N-50G1-JDPT-T1Y1-00000-00&context=1519360), [NBC - 8 WFLA](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D0P-MVT1-JBCN-32J5-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Ongoing law enforcement investigations and special security measures highlight the seriousness of threats, but could also be seen as a distraction from legislative work or political messaging ([Islander News](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6D0R-MDM1-DXVP-T0T3-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Accusations of past misuse of legal processes and personal allegations (including military regulation violations) may fuel opposition narratives about Luna's judgment or motivations ([American Action News](https://americanactionnews.com/media/2021/10/18/anna-paulina-lunas-150000-dirty-trick-rs-fg/)).

### Petitions Alleging Threats or Murder Plots

Luna lost a congressional race to Crist last year. She said two other Republicans, Matt Tito and Amanda Makki, were also in on the plot." \[WMNF, [6/16/21](https://www.wmnf.org/florida-gop-congressional-candidate-says-republican-opponent-conspired-to-kill-her/)\]

**2021: Anna Paulina Luna Filed Complaint Alleging Murder Plot By Opponents** According to a blog post by Francisco Gonzalez on American Action News, "Earlier this year, Luna...filed an extraordinary and sensational claim with the St. Petersburg Police Department and the State's Attorney. She accused all of her prospective opponents in the upcoming primary contest for the Republican nomination for Congress in the 13th District...of plotting to murder her!" \[Blog Post - Francisco Gonzalez on American Action News, [10/18/21](https://americanactionnews.com/media/2021/10/18/anna-paulina-lunas-150000-dirty-trick-rs-fg/)\]
