# Education and Military Service

### Summary

-   Anna Paulina Luna enlisted in the U.S. Air Force at age 19 in 2009 and served as an airfield management specialist for five years, earning the rank of Senior Airman, an Air Force Achievement Medal, and an honorable discharge in 2014 ([Tampa Bay Times](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:611W-CYD1-DYTM-N0H1-00000-00&context=1519360), [Washington Post](https://www.washingtonpost.com/politics/2022/11/08/anna-paulina-luna-congress-instagram-maga/), [Yahoo](https://www.yahoo.com/news/anna-paulina-luna-husband-andrew-232442483.html)).

-   After her active duty service, Luna spent a year in the Air National Guard, including a relocation to Oregon to serve alongside her husband ([Tampa Bay Times](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66M0-2CX1-JCBJ-Y32B-00000-00&context=1519360), [Yahoo](https://www.yahoo.com/news/anna-paulina-luna-husband-andrew-232442483.html)).

-   Luna earned a Bachelor of Science in biology from the University of West Florida, crediting her military service and the GI Bill for funding her education ([Fox 13 News](https://www.fox13news.com/news/rising-gop-star-anna-paulina-luna-handily-wins-primary-faces-charlie-crist-in-november), [Tampa Bay Times](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:611W-CYD1-DYTM-N0H1-00000-00&context=1519360), [El Nuevo Herald](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6F17-3213-RRTS-M2GP-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Luna has publicly discussed overcoming challenges to complete her high school education through an adult education program due to frequent relocations during her upbringing ([TIME](https://time.com/6292871/anna-paulina-luna-influencer-congress/), [voteannapaulina.com](https://www.voteannapaulina.com/post/the-influencer-who-came-to-congress)).

-   Possible vulnerabilities in Luna's account include questions regarding her description of a traumatic home invasion during her military service, which is disputed by a former roommate, who confirmed only a break-in when neither was present ([Stars and Stripes](https://www.stripes.com/theaters/us/2023-02-10/making-congresswoman-anna-paulina-luna-9100015.html)).

-   Luna has faced scrutiny over her employment during military service, notably being confronted about alleged work as a stripper while in the Air Force, which she partially addressed in interviews ([Central Florida Post](https://centralfloridapost.com/2022/05/11/anna-paulina-luna-has-decades-long-history-of-abusing-court-issued-protective-orders/), [Florida Politics](https://floridapolitics.com/archives/455163-a-tough-conversation-with-anna-paulina-luna/)).
