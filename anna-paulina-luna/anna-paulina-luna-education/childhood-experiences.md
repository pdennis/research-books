# Childhood Experiences

### Summary

-   Anna Paulina Luna grew up in a family that relied on welfare, according to both family members and multiple sources ([TIME](https://time.com/6292871/anna-paulina-luna-influencer-congress/), [voteannapaulina.com](https://www.voteannapaulina.com/post/the-influencer-who-came-to-congress)).

-   She joined the military specifically to help pay for her education ([TIME](https://time.com/6292871/anna-paulina-luna-influencer-congress/), [voteannapaulina.com](https://www.voteannapaulina.com/post/the-influencer-who-came-to-congress)).

-   Luna has described her childhood as "harrowing," indicating she experienced significant difficulties growing up ([Stars and Stripes](https://www.stripes.com/theaters/us/2023-02-10/making-congresswoman-anna-paulina-luna-9100015.html)).

-   She credits her challenging upbringing with making her "battle hardened," a theme she cited during her campaign ([Stars and Stripes](https://www.stripes.com/theaters/us/2023-02-10/making-congresswoman-anna-paulina-luna-9100015.html)).

-   Luna's background and difficult childhood have influenced her political views, including strong advocacy for gun rights ([Stars and Stripes](https://www.stripes.com/theaters/us/2023-02-10/making-congresswoman-anna-paulina-luna-9100015.html)).
