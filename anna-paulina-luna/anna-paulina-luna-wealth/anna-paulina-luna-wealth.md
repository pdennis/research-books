# Overview of Anna Paulina Luna's Wealth

### Summary

-   Anna Paulina Luna has raised significant campaign funds over recent election cycles, surpassing \$1 million in 2021 and leading all candidates in fundraising for the 2022 13th District congressional race ([Tampa Bay Times, 2/1/22](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:64NY-MDH1-DYTM-N1V4-00000-00&context=1519360), [Tampa Bay Times, 6/7/22](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:65MT-T201-JCBJ-Y019-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Luna's campaign spending is high, often exceeding 70% of funds raised in quarterly periods, which has left her with comparatively lower cash on hand than some rivals ([Tampa Bay Times, 2/1/22](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:64NY-MDH1-DYTM-N1V4-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   In 2023, Luna raised \$957,357 and had \$559,148 cash on hand as of September 30, showing ongoing fundraising strength ([Tampa Bay Times, 1/16/24](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6B46-TKP1-JCBJ-Y00R-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   In 2024, Luna disclosed an investment of between \$200,000 and \$450,000 in America First Natural Resources LLC, a company owned by political supporter Bruce Rosenthal, with this investment making up a major share of her disclosed assets ([Miami Herald, 7/1/24](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CCN-47M1-JC3J-X012-00000-00&context=1519360), [Tampa Bay Times, 7/2/24](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CD1-RXH1-JCBJ-Y0MF-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Luna has received campaign contributions from iGas-affiliated companies and employees, as well as from Bruce Rosenthal, who has donated over \$40,000 to political committees supporting her since 2020 ([Miami Herald, 11/16/23](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:69N1-XYP1-JC3J-X3W4-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Luna's significant financial ties to supporters and related business ventures could present potential conflicts of interest, drawing scrutiny from ethics experts ([Miami Herald, 7/1/24](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6CCN-47M1-JC3J-X012-00000-00&context=1519360)).
