# Pre-Congressional Activities and Media Work

### Summary

-   Anna Paulina Luna built her profile as a conservative commentator and activist prior to entering Congress, notably working with Turning Point USA and PragerU, where she held leadership roles focused on Hispanic outreach and initiative development ([Tampa Bay Times](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:66M0-2CX1-JCBJ-Y32B-00000-00&context=1519360), [TIME](https://time.com/6292871/anna-paulina-luna-influencer-congress/)).

-   She gained additional media exposure through appearances and commentary for outlets like Fox News, Newsmax, and El American, where she sometimes promoted right-wing talking points and election misinformation ([Media Matters](https://www.mediamatters.org/spanish-language-media/between-congressional-bids-el-americans-anna-paulina-luna-has-been), [Tampa Bay Times](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6677-J7K1-DYTM-N43V-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Luna cultivated a strong social media presence and engaged in grassroots activism, organizing rallies and working with groups such as Bienvenido to mobilize Hispanic voters ([RationalWiki](https://rationalwiki.org/wiki/Anna_Paulina_Luna), [TheDPLG](https://www.thedplg.com/celebs-wild/gaze-upon-anna-paulina-lunas-captivating-feet.html)).

-   Her background also includes work as a model, featuring in publications like Maxim and Sports Illustrated, and involvement in viral media such as a MAGA swimsuit video, which resurfaced and drew both support and criticism ([Tribune](https://tribune.com.pk/story/2488338/anna-paulina-luna-addresses-viral-maga-swimsuit-video), [Tampa Bay Times](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:611W-CYD1-DYTM-N0H1-00000-00&context=1519360)).

-   Potential controversies include reports and ongoing debate over her past employment at a gentlemen's club while in the Air Force, with conflicting accounts as to whether she worked as a dancer or a cocktail waitress ([Central Florida Post](https://centralfloridapost.com/2021/12/14/anna-paulina-luna-gone-wild-lies-plague-campaign-of-former-stripper/), [Orlando Local News](https://orlandolocalnews.com/former-stripper-anna-paulina-luna-mayerhoffer-to-join-dc-draino-for-tpusa-pep-rally)).
