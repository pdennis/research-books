# International Relations and Foreign Aid

### Summary

-   Rep. Anna Paulina Luna has consistently opposed additional U.S. aid to Ukraine, citing concerns about accountability, transparency, and prioritizing domestic issues over foreign assistance ([CNN](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6BV1-KWD1-F15W-H01J-00000-00), [Fox Business](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6793-9DB1-JDPT-T322-00000-00)).

-   Luna has advocated for auditing existing U.S. support to Ukraine and criticized the scale of current aid, arguing it could have funded significant domestic programs and expressing skepticism after a visit with Ukrainian officials ([CNN](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6BV1-KWD1-F15W-H01J-00000-00), [Fox Business](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6795-9GK1-JBHT-D1W4-00000-00)).

-   She has pushed back against packages that send aid to both Ukraine and entities connected to the Israel-Hamas conflict, and voiced opposition to U.S. funding for the Palestinian Authority due to links with Hamas ([CNN](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6BV1-KWD1-F15W-H01J-00000-00), [The Free Press](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6BBN-3RG1-DXVP-T52Y-00000-00)).

-   Luna has criticized the UN Human Rights Council for what she describes as anti-Israel bias and ignoring abuses by countries like China, introducing legislation to block U.S. funding until the UN condemns Hamas ([The Free Press](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:69K5-1WD1-JBCN-3232-00000-00)).

-   She supports "America First" policies such as reciprocal tariffs to boost U.S. manufacturing and expressed openness to U.S. territorial expansion in Greenland as a strategic interest ([Fox News](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6FFD-P9K3-S1C1-70JM-00000-00)).

-   Luna's strong opposition to Ukraine funding has drawn criticism from some constituents and commentators, who argue her stance undermines U.S. leadership and support for allies ([Tampa Bay Times](https://advance.lexis.com/api/document?collection=news&id=urn:contentItem:6BP2-FR21-DYTM-N3T9-00000-00)).
